#### Certification

From BITSoM and PwC Academy

Module 1: Digital Finance Blueprint: Core Concepts and Disruption

- • Core finance concepts: Risk vs. Return, Discounting, and Compounding principles.
- • Overview of Financial Statements and Fundamental Analysis.
- • Introduction to Financial Markets: Key instruments (equity, debt, derivatives) and market functions.
- • Defining FinTech, its evolution, and primary domains (Payments, Lending, Wealth).

Module 2: FinTech Data Accelerator: Toolkit Mastery (Excel, Python, SQL, Viz)

- • Financial Modeling with Excel: Time Value of Money, basic financial functions, and data manipulation
- • Python Programming Basics: Introduction to Python, data types, control flow, functions, and key libraries (NumPy, Pandas)
- • Introduction to SQL and Data Access: Database concepts, basic querying (SELECT, WHERE, JOIN), and data extraction logic
- • Data Visualization with Tableau: Principles of visual communication, dashboard design, and effective data storytelling
- • Practical Tools: Excel / Google Sheets, Python, Basic SQL, Tableau

Module 3: The API Economy: Payments, Lending, and Open Banking

- • Payment Infrastructure: UPI, digital wallets, instant payment rails, and payment gateways
- • Lending Innovation: Peer-to-Peer (P2P) lending, Buy Now Pay Later (BNPL) models, and modern credit scoring logic
- • Open Banking and API-driven finance: Data-sharing frameworks and platform innovation
- • Practical Tools: Postman (for API testing concepts)

Module 4: Machine Learning Fundamentals: The Analytical Engine

- • Data Preparation in Finance: Structured vs. unstructured data, cleaning, and feature engineering
- • Descriptive Statistics and Probability for Financial Data
- • Machine Learning Fundamentals: Supervised Learning (Classification, Regression, and core model assumptions
- • Model Evaluation: Key performance metrics (Accuracy, ROC-AUC, Precision, Recall)
- • Practical Tools: Utilizing Python skills for theoretical application

Module 5: Intelligent Applications: AI for Risk, Fraud, and Growth

- • Risk & Fraud: Advanced credit scoring and real-time Anomaly Detection for fraud monitoring
- • InsurTech: AI applications in insurance (underwriting, claims automation, and risk assessment)
- • Client Engagement: AI in customer service (chatbots, virtual assistants) and personalization/recommendation engines
- • Financial Risk Overview: How AI addresses credit risk, liquidity risk, and market risk
- • Large language models and Agentic AI: the new frontier
- • Practical Tools: Python (advanced Scikit-learn), Introduction to basic NLP libraries for labs

Module 6: The Future Stack: DLT, Governance, and Strategic Trends

- • Digital Ledger Technology (DLT): Blockchain Fundamentals, Cryptographic Hashing, Smart Contracts, and an overview of DeFi/Digital Assets
- • Regulatory Technology (RegTech): KYC/AML automation, compliance reporting, and data governance
- • Cybersecurity: Identifying common FinTech threats and defensive measures
- • Future Strategy: Analysis of Central Bank Digital Currencies (CBDCs), Sustainable Finance (ESG), and the strategic role of Generative AI in financial workflows
- • Practical Tools: Online Blockchain Sandbox, PowerPoint/Miro (for business strategy)

Module 7: FinTech Product Studio: Integrated Capstone Project

- • Problem Definition and Project Scoping
- • Data Analysis and Model Implementation
- • Development of Strategic Recommendation and Business Plan
- • Professional Presentation and Defense
- • Integrated Tools: Holistic use of Python, Excel, SQL, Tableau, and presentation software

## FAQs

### Programme Overview

**Q: What is the program duration?**

The program spans <strong>6 months</strong>, offering a structured and immersive learning journey designed to build strong expertise at the intersection of <strong>Fintech and Artificial Intelligence</strong>.

**Q: What topics and concepts are covered in the program?**

The BITSoM <strong>Executive Certification in Fintech and Artificial Intelligence</strong>, in collaboration with Masai School, covers both foundational and advanced concepts in financial technology and AI. Key areas include digital payments, blockchain, robo-advisory, fraud detection, algorithmic trading, credit risk modeling, and regulatory technology (RegTech). Alongside, the curriculum emphasizes AI-driven applications such as machine learning for finance, natural language processing for financial data, and generative AI for customer engagement.

**Q: What are the projects I will work on?**

You will work on <strong>industry-relevant projects</strong> such as building credit scoring models, developing fraud detection systems, designing robo-advisory solutions, creating blockchain-based applications, and applying AI to financial forecasting and sentiment analysis. (Projects are subject to change.) These projects are designed to provide hands-on exposure to solving real-world Fintech challenges.

**Q: Will I receive a digital or physical copy of the programme certificate?**

Upon successful completion of the program and fulfillment of all requirements, participants will receive a digital copy of the certificate. The digital certificate will be dispatched to the registered address shared during enrollment.

**Q: When will I receive my certificate after completing the program?**

Certificates will be issued within 2–3 months from the date of the final examination. The exact timeline for issuance will be communicated during the program.

**Q: What is the mode of delivery for the program?**

The program is delivered entirely online via Zoom, enabling you to learn from anywhere while engaging in live, interactive sessions.

**Q: Who will be teaching the classes in this program?**

The sessions will be led by BITSoM faculty members with academic and research expertise in finance, technology, and AI. In addition, seasoned industry practitioners from banking, Fintech startups, and AI-driven enterprises will conduct applied sessions, ensuring a rich blend of theoretical and practical insights.

**Q: What tools and software will I learn during the course?**

You will gain experience with industry-standard tools such as Python, Excel, Power BI, SQL enabling practical applications in finance, and AI-driven solutions.

**Q: Will I work on real-world case studies?**

Yes, the program integrates <strong>real-world case studies</strong> from banking, insurance, investment management, and Fintech domains. These case studies will help you understand how AI is transforming the financial industry and allow you to practice applying your knowledge to real-world business scenarios.

**Q: What is campus immersion?**

The 2-day campus immersion is an optional in-person immersive experience designed to enhance learning through instructor interaction, campus exposure, and networking opportunities. The immersion may include AI-focused sessions, lab visits, faculty interactions, and curated academic engagements.

**Q: Is campus immersion mandatory, and when does it take place?**

It is typically scheduled towards the end of the program or after completion. While not mandatory, participation is highly recommended. The schedule will be shared in advance to enable travel planning.

**Q: What is the fee for the campus immersion?**

The campus immersion fee is ₹6,000 for Non-Residential participation and ₹10,000 for Residential participation per day. This fee covers one day of stay for residential participants and includes lunch and refreshments provided during the scheduled immersion hours.<br><br>The immersion fee is not included in the programme fee and must be paid separately by participants. Travel expenses are not included and must be arranged and borne individually by the participants.

### Class Schedule

**Q: What are the class timings?**

The classes are scheduled on weekdays as well as weekends, designed with your time and priorities in mind. On weekdays, classes are scheduled from 8:00PM onwards and weekend timings are subject to professor’s availability and schedule. The detailed schedule will be shared with you on Day 1 of the program.

**Q: How many hours of engagement is required per week?**

You’ll need to dedicate approximately 7–8 hours per week, which includes attending live sessions(3-4 hours), completing tutorials, and engaging in self-paced study to stay on track with the program.

**Q: How will the class schedule be communicated to students?**

The class schedule will be communicated primarily through the Learning Management System (LMS). It will also be shared on the official WhatsApp community.

**Q: What if I miss a live session , will I be able to access recording?**

We recommend you to attend Live sessions. In any foreseen circumstances if you miss attending live classes, recordings will be made accessible to you on the Learning Management System (LMS). Kindly ensure to watch the entire class recording to ensure you don't miss important concepts covered.

**Q: I am a working professional / college student and would like to plan my week ahead. Can I get access to the schedule well in advance?**

Yes, you will receive the class schedule and updates well in advance, allowing you to plan your time effectively and stay organized.

**Q: Is there any attendance criteria in this program for course completion?**

Yes, to be eligible for course completion, you must maintain a minimum of 65% attendance, which can be achieved through either live sessions or recorded lectures.

**Q: How is attendance calculated?**

Attendance is calculated automatically through the Learning Management System (LMS). You will be marked present if you complete either one of the following in full:<br><br><strong>Live Attendance</strong> – Counted when you join the live session in full using your registered email ID and attend the class.<br><br><strong>Recording Attendance</strong> – Counted when you watch the session recording in full within 30 days of the class.

**Q: What happens if the student doesn’t meet the required attendance criteria?**

If a student does not meet the required attendance criteria, they will not be eligible to receive the course completion certificate.

**Q: What if I am unable to attend live sessions or watch recordings within 30 days due to some exigencies?**

In such cases, you can raise a support ticket with your respective Program Coordinator, along with the necessary supporting documents. Exceptions may be granted after reviewing your request.

Once your enrolment is complete, you will be onboarded on the Learning Management System (LMS). You can raise your queries through ticketing support and your program coordinator support with all non-conceptual queries during the tenure of the course.

**Q: How can I interact with my peers?**

You will have opportunities to engage throughout the program. You can connect with industry mentors and TAs during dedicated doubt-clearing sessions. Additionally, you can interact and collaborate with fellow learners through our learning platforms and private Discord community.

**Q: Is there a helpline session for student queries?**

 Yes, a dedicated live helpline session is available to address your queries. Details of the session will be shared as part of your weekly schedule to help you stay informed and supported throughout the program.

You will have access to a variety of learning resources, like notes, pre-reads, class recordings, assignments to support learning on Learning Management System (LMS).
