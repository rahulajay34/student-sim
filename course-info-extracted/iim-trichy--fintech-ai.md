Module 1: Foundations of FinTech in Banking & Finance

- • Back to Basics – Banking Meets FinTech
- • Money in Motion – Digital Payments & Settlements

- • Smart Markets – Trading & Investment Innovation
- • New Frontiers – Alternative Assets & Risks
- • Compliance by Design – RegTech & Monitoring

Goal: To explore how FinTech innovations are transforming financial markets while examining the regulatory and compliance landscape and challenges.

- • Data Analysis using Excel
- • Data Analysis using SQL
- • Data Analysis using Power BI
- • Data Analysis using Python
Goal: To develop practical skills in preparing, analyzing, and visualizing financial data using industry-standard analytical tools to support FinTech decision-making and innovation.

- • AI & ML Foundations
- • Machine Learning for Credit Risk & Fraud Detection
- • Machine Learning for Customer Insights
- • Introduction to GenAI
Goal: To introduce the foundational concepts of Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning, develop familiarity with key techniques, and map their relevance to financial services, banking, markets, and FinTech innovation.

Module 5: Capstone Project Presentations

## FAQs

### Program Overview

**Q: What is the program duration?**

The program spans 8 months, offering a structured and immersive journey to equip you with in-demand FinTech and AI skills.

**Q: Will I receive a digital or physical copy of the programme certificate?**

Upon successful completion of the program and fulfillment of all academic requirements, participants will receive a physical copy of the certificate. The physical certificate will be dispatched to the registered address of the participant.

**Q: When will I receive my certificate after completing the program?**

Certificates will be issued within 2–3 months from the date of the final examination. The exact timeline for issuance will be communicated during the program.

**Q: What is the mode of delivery for the program?**

The program is delivered online via Zoom, with live interactive sessions, practical workshops, and dedicated project guidance.

**Q: Who will be teaching the classes in this program?**

Classes are led by IIM Trichy faculty experts in FinTech, banking, and AI.

**Q: Is there an in-person component?**

Yes, the program includes a 2-day mandatory campus immersion at IIM Trichy, where you will interact with faculty, collaborate with peers, and experience hands-on learning in an intensive in-person setting

**Q: What is campus immersion?**

The campus immersion is a 2-day academic experience designed to enhance learning through instructor interaction, campus exposure and networking opportunities. The immersion may include faculty-led classes, formal academic engagements, peer interaction, and the graduation ceremony.

**Q: Is campus immersion mandatory, and when does it take place?**

It is typically scheduled towards the end of the program or after completion. Participation is mandatory as part of the program requirements (subject to applicable attendance criteria). The schedule will be shared in advance to enable travel planning.

**Q: What is the fee for the campus immersion?**

The immersion fee is approximately ₹10,000–₹12,000 for 2 days (as applicable). This amount is not included in the course fees and must be borne additionally by students.<br><br>This fee includes lunch and refreshments provided during the scheduled immersion hours. Accommodation may be available, subject to availability and applicable charges. Travel expenses must be borne by students and are not included in the course fees.

### Eligibility & Admissions

**Q: What are the eligibility criteria for the program?**

Anyone with a minimum qualification of Graduation (or equivalent) and a keen interest in building a career in FinTech, banking, or financial technology innovation is eligible to apply.

**Q: Can international students apply for the program?**

Yes, international students can apply, provided they meet the eligibility criteria for the course.

**Q: What is the admission process?**

The process begins with signing up and submitting your Statement of Work (SOW) along with the application form. Shortlisted candidates are invited for a one-on-one session to discuss the program overview and guide you through the next steps for smooth enrollment.

**Q: What skills does the SOW and Application Process assess?**

The SOW and application evaluate your analytical thinking, problem-solving skills, financial curiosity, and understanding of emerging FinTech trends. It also assesses your ability to apply practical reasoning to real-world financial scenarios.

### Fees

**Q: What is the difference between Registration Fee and Course Fee?**

The Registration Fee secures your seat in the program. The Course Fee is the total program cost, payable upfront or in EMIs via our NBFC partners. Enrollment is confirmed only after the full Course Fee is paid.

**Q: How soon will I receive the receipt after payment?**

Receipts are emailed to your registered address within 24–48 business hours of payment.

**Q: Are there any financial institutions/partners involved in the EMI process?**

Yes, Masai School partners with trusted NBFCs such as Bajaj Finance, ShopSe, Varthana, and Propelld to facilitate EMI payments.

**Q: What is the fee payment deadline?**

The deadline is displayed on your dashboard. Please ensure the payment is completed within the timeline to confirm your enrollment.

**Q: Is there any financial aid or scholarship available?**

No, this program does not offer scholarships.

**Q: What is the rate of interest on the loan?**

Interest rates vary based on factors like tenure, amount, credit score, and the NBFC partner. Specific details will be provided by the finance team.

### Class Schedule

**Q: What are the class timings?**

The classes are scheduled on weekends, designed with your time and priorities in mind. The detailed schedule will be shared with you on Day 1 of the program.

**Q: How many hours of engagement is required per week?**

You'll need to dedicate approximately 5–9 hours per week, which includes attending live sessions of 3 hours, completing tutorials, and engaging in self-paced study to stay on track with the program.

**Q: How will the class schedule be communicated to students?**

The class schedule will be communicated primarily through the Masai Learning Management System (LMS). It will also be shared on the official WhatsApp community.

**Q: What if I miss a live session , will I be able to access recording?**

We recommend you to attend Live sessions. In any foreseen circumstances if you miss attending live classes, recordings will be made accessible to you on the Masai Learning Management System (LMS) for upto 30 days. Kindly ensure to watch the entire class recording to ensure you don't miss important concepts covered.

**Q: I am a working professional / college student and would like to plan my week ahead. Can I get access to the schedule well in advance?**

Yes, you'll receive the class schedule and updates every Saturday for the upcoming week, allowing you to plan your time effectively and stay organized.

**Q: Is attendance mandatory for course completion?**

Yes, attendance is mandatory. You must attend at least 75% of the live sessions to successfully complete the program and receive the certificate.

### Student Support

**Q: How can I raise any queries once I enroll in the program?**

Once your enrolment is complete, you will be onboarded on the Masai Learning Management System (LMS). You can raise your queries through ticketing support and your program coordinator support with all non-conceptual queries during the tenure of the course.

**Q: How can I interact with my peers?**

You will have opportunities to engage throughout the program. You can connect with industry mentors and TAs during dedicated doubt-clearing sessions. Additionally, you can interact and collaborate with fellow learners through our learning platforms and private Discord community.

**Q: Is there a helpline session for student queries?**

Yes, a dedicated live helpline session is available to address your queries. Details of the session will be shared as part of your weekly schedule to help you stay informed and supported throughout the program.

**Q: What learning resources will be made available during the program?**

You will have access to a variety of learning resources, like notes, pre-reads, class recordings, assignments to support learning on Masai Learning Management System (LMS).

**Q: How often will assessments be conducted?**

Assessments will be conducted periodically throughout the program, with evaluations at the end of each module and ongoing project work to track your progress.

**Q: What is the criteria for earning a certificate?**

Participants must score a minimum of 40% in cummilative evaluations and maintain 75% attendance in live sessions to earn the certificate of completion.

All industry-readiness support, including AI-powered tools, mock interviews, mentor-led guidance, and personal branding sessions—is offered exclusively by Masai.

Industry readiness means being equipped with the right skills, network, and interview preparation to launch your career. Our program helps you build practical expertise, connect with a 20K+ peer and mentor community, and prepare with AI-driven tools. Alongside, you’ll gain practical skills in this course through projects and industry-relevant tools to stand out in the job market (Eligibility: Minimum 50% marks in this program).
