Module-1: Ideation & Opportunity (ENTR101)

- • Cultivate entrepreneurial mindset and creativity
- • Spot and validate real market problems
- • Practice idea generation and brainstorming
- • Test problem-solution fit
- • Craft a concise elevator pitch

Module 2: Market Research & Validation (ENTR102)

- • Conduct customer interviews and surveys
- • Map target segments and market size
- • Create personas and empathy maps
- • Design and run MVP experiments
- • Use data to decide pivot or persevere

Module 3: Business Model, Finance & Legal (ENTR103)

- • Build Business Model Canvas and value proposition
- • Understand revenue models and pricing
- • Draft basic financial projections
- • Learn startup legal structures and IP basics
- • Prepare key startup documents and agreements

Module 4: Product Development & MVP Execution (ENTR104)

- • Build no-code MVPs and run usability tests
- • Apply design thinking and UX fundamentals
- • Iterate to achieve product-market fit
- • Learn rapid prototyping techniques
- • Use startup tools for development and IP protection

Module 5: Marketing & Growth (ENTR105)

- • Craft go-to-market and growth plans
- • Run digital marketing and social media campaigns
- • Apply growth hacking and viral marketing techniques
- • Track key metrics and run A/B tests
- • Build retention and community engagement strategies

Module 6: Capstone – Launch & Pitch (ENTR106)

- • Refine MVP and prepare investor pitch deck
- • Practice public speaking and pitch delivery
- • Present demo and receive expert feedback
- • Map post-program milestones and growth steps

## FAQs

### Program Overview

**Q: What is the program duration?**

The program spans four months offering an intensive and structured learning experience on how to build your own venture.

**Q: Will I receive a certificate upon completion?**

Yes, upon successful completion of the program and based on your performance, you will receive a certificate from Vishlesan i-Hub, IIT Patna.

**Q: What is the mode of delivery for the program?**

The program is delivered entirely online through Zoom platform, allowing you to learn flexibly from anywhere.

**Q: Who will be teaching the classes in this program?**

The sessions will be led by experienced faculty members and Industry Experts who have deep expertise in Entrepreneurship, Leadership, and Innovation. Alongside them, you'll learn from accomplished Startup Founders, Investors, and Industry Leaders ensuring a comprehensive and practical learning experience rooted with real-world insights.

**Q: What are the benefits of attending this program?**

This program helps you with a roadmap to transform your idea into a successful startup. You will master business strategy, product development, and innovation, work on real-world projects, and strengthen your leadership skills. Guided by our esteemed faculty and industry experts, you'll learn how to validate ideas, develop an MVP, and create a pitch deck. The program concludes with Pitch Day, where top teams pitch their startups to investors.

### Eligibility & Admissions

**Q: What are the eligibility criteria for the program?**

This program is designed for college students, working professionals and aspiring entrepreneurs.

**Q: Can international students apply for the program?**

Yes, international students can apply for the program, provided they meet the eligibility criteria set for the course.

**Q: What is the admission process?**

The admissions process begins with signing up and taking a Qualifier test. If you clear the test, you'll be invited for a one-on-one session where we'll walk you through the program overview and share the next steps to help you complete your enrollment smoothly.

**Q: What skills does the Qualifier test assess?**

The Qualifier test evaluates your vocabulary in business contexts, motivation and entrepreneurial mindset, logical thinking and business math, general awareness, and your ability to apply concepts through a mini case study  all of which are essential foundations for the program.

**Q: Can I take the Qualifier test after my college exams are over?**

Seats are limited, so we strongly recommend taking the Qualifier test at the earliest to avoid missing the opportunity to kickstart your career as an Entrepreneur.

### Program Fee

**Q: What is the difference between Registration fee and Course fees?**

The Registration Fee secures your seat in the program. The Course Fee is the total program cost, which can be paid upfront or in EMIs with support from our NBFC partner. Your enrollment is confirmed only after the full Course Fee is paid.

**Q: How soon will I receive the receipt after paying the registration or course fee?**

The receipt will be emailed to your registered address within 24–48 business hours of payment.

**Q: Are there any specific financial institutions/partners involved in the EMI process?**

Yes, we've partnered with trusted NBFCs like Bajaj Finance, ShopSe, Varthana, Propelld, to support you with the EMI process.

**Q: What is the fee payment deadline?**

The fee payment deadline is available on your dashboard. Kindly ensure to complete the fee payment within the timelines to complete your enrolment in the program.

**Q: Is there any financial aid or scholarship available?**

Yes, scholarships are offered based on your performance in the Qualifier test. The program reserves the right to determine the eligibility and extent of the scholarship awarded to each candidate.

**Q: What is the rate of interest on the loan?**

The rate of interest depends upon multiple factors like tenure, amount, credit score, NBFC partner etc. The details will be informed by the finance team for the program.

### Class Schedule

**Q: What are the class timings?**

The classes are scheduled on weekdays as well as weekends, designed with your time and priorities in mind. On weekdays ,classes are scheduled from 8:00PM onwards and weekend timings are subject to professor's availability and schedule. The detailed schedule will be shared with you on Day 1 of the program.

**Q: How many hours of engagement is required per week?**

You'll need to dedicate approximately 8–9 hours per week, which includes attending live sessions, completing tutorials, and engaging in self-paced study to stay on track with the program.

**Q: How will the class schedule be communicated to students?**

The class schedule will be communicated primarily through the Learning Management System (LMS). It will also be shared on the official WhatsApp community.

**Q: What if I miss a live session , will I be able to access recording?**

We recommend you to attend Live sessions. In any foreseen circumstances if you miss attending live classes, recordings will be made accessible to you on the Learning Management System (LMS). Kindly ensure to watch the entire class recording to ensure you don't miss important concepts covered.

**Q: I am a working professional / college student and would like to plan my week ahead. Can I get access to the schedule well in advance?**

Yes, you'll receive the class schedule and updates every Saturday for the upcoming week, allowing you to plan your time effectively and stay organized.

**Q:  Is Attendance mandatory for course completion?**

While we strongly recommend attending all live sessions to make the most of the program. In cases where you miss live classes,, it's essential to watch the complete class recordings to stay on track and ensure you grasp the concepts covered. Consistent participation is important for successful course completion and getting the best learning experience.

### Student Support

**Q: How can I raise any queries once I enroll in the program?**

Once your enrolment is complete, you will be onboarded on Learning Management System (LMS). You can raise your queries through ticketing support and your program coordinator support with all non-conceptual queries during the tenure of the course.

**Q: How can I interact with my peers?**

You will have opportunities to engage throughout the program. You can connect with industry mentors and TAs during dedicated doubt-clearing sessions. Additionally, you can interact and collaborate with fellow learners through our learning platforms and private Discord community.

**Q: Is there a helpline session for student queries?**

Yes, a dedicated live helpline session is available to address your queries. Details of the session will be shared as part of your weekly schedule to help you stay informed and supported throughout the program.

**Q: What learning resources will be made available during the program?**

You will have access to a variety of learning resources, like notes, pre-reads, class recordings, assignments to support learning on Learning Management System (LMS).

**Q: How often will assessments be conducted?**

Assessments will be conducted periodically throughout the program, with evaluations at the end of each module and ongoing project work to track your progress.

**Q: What happens if I don't clear a module?**

The program follows a milestone-based approach rather than traditional grading. Progress is evaluated through hands-on deliverables like your MVP one-pager, working prototype, and final product demo. If you're unable to complete a milestone on time, you'll be guided by mentors during weekly sessions to catch up. Active participation, consistent effort, and completing key milestones are essential to successfully complete the program and receive certification.

### Placement Opportunities

**Q: Does this program offer placement support?**

This program is not designed for job placements. Instead, it prepares you to become a job creator. Through mentorship, startup simulations, and exposure to real-world entrepreneurial challenges, you'll build the skills and mindset needed to launch ventures or lead innovation-driven roles in dynamic environments.

**Q: Is this program still relevant if I'm not starting a business immediately?**

Absolutely. The program equips you with essential skills such as business decision-making, strategic thinking, and risk management skills that are highly valuable across roles in startups, corporates, or future entrepreneurial initiatives.

**Q: What are the key outcomes I can expect from this program?**

You will complete the program with a well-developed Founder's Portfolio, practical experience in startup simulations, and access to a network of experienced mentors and peers. These outcomes are designed to strengthen your entrepreneurial profile and prepare you for real-world business challenge
