# What Will You Learn?

This program covers the foundations of analytics, including data cleaning and analysis using spreadsheets, SQL for querying data, and Tableau for dashboard creation and storytelling. Learners will also explore GenAI basics and its application in analytics workflows, along with basic Python and pandas for simple data preparation and transformation. The course concludes with a mini-project showcasing an end-to-end analytics workflow and insight communication.

## FAQs

### Program Overview

**Q: What is the program duration?**

The program spans 04  months, offering a structured and immersive learning journey, it includes modules with lectures, tutorials, practicals, and self-study components, indicating a multi-month comprehensive structure.

**Q: What is the mode of delivery for the program?**

The mode of delivery is online through the Zoom platform. 

**Q: Who will be teaching the classes in this program?**

The sessions will be held in Hindi and English  and led by esteemed faculty members who bring strong academic and research expertise in the domains. Alongside them, seasoned industry experts will conduct sessions to provide practical insights and real-world applications ensuring a comprehensive and hands-on learning experience.

**Q: Will I receive a digital or physical copy of the programme certificate?**

Upon successful completion of the program and fulfillment of all academic requirements, participants will receive a digital copy of the certificate.

**Q: When will I receive my certificate after completing the program?**

Certificates will be issued within 2–3 months from the date of the final examination. The exact timeline for issuance will be communicated during the program.

### Eligibility & Admissions

**Q: What are the eligibility criteria for the program?**

 Anyone with a minimum qualification of 12th grade (or equivalent) and a strong interest in building a career in Data Analytics and Gen AI. A basic understanding of the concepts is recommended, but not mandatory

**Q: Can international students apply for the program?**

Yes, international students can apply, provided they meet the eligibility criteria set for the course. Specific criteria are not detailed in the document.

**Q: What is the admission process?**

The admission process begins with signing up and taking a Qualifier test. If you clear the test, you’ll be invited for a one-on-one session where the program overview and next steps for enrollment will be shared to complete the process smoothly.

**Q: What skills does the Qualifier test assess?**

The Qualifier test evaluates your Quantitative Aptitude, Logical Reasoning, Data Interpretation, and Verbal Ability, which are basic requirements for the program.

**Q: Can I take the Qualifier test after my college exams are over?**

Seats are limited, so we strongly recommend taking the Qualifier test at the earliest to avoid missing the opportunity to kickstart your career in Data Analytics and Generative AI.

### Fees

**Q: What is the difference between the Registration fee and the Course fees?**

The Registration Fee secures your seat in the program. The Course Fee is the total program cost, which can be paid upfront or in EMIs with support of NBFCs partnered with Masai School, the Ed-tech and Implementation partner for this program. Your enrollment is confirmed only after the full Course Fee is paid. 

**Q: How soon will I receive the receipt after paying the registration or course fee?**

The receipt will be emailed to your registered address within 24–48 business hours of payment.

**Q: Are there any specific financial institutions/partners involved in the EMI process?**

Yes, Masai School, the Ed-tech and implementation partner for this program is associated with trusted NBFCs like Bajaj Finance, ShopSe, Varthana, Propelld, to support you with the EMI process.

**Q: What is the fee payment deadline?**

The fee payment deadline is available on your dashboard. Kindly ensure to complete the fee payment within the timelines to complete your enrolment in the program.

**Q: Is there any financial aid or scholarship available?**

Yes, scholarships are offered based on your performance in the Qualifier test. The program reserves the right to determine the eligibility and extent of the scholarship awarded to each candidate.

**Q: What is the rate of interest on the loan?**

The rate of interest depends upon multiple factors like tenure, amount, credit score, NBFC partner, etc. The details will be provided by the finance team for the program.

**Q: Is there any additional cost for admission kits?**

The kit cost of ₹199 is already included in the total course fee. There are no additional charges for admission kits.

**Q: Are there any additional charges for assessments during the course?**

The assessment cost of ₹800, which covers all assessments throughout the course, is already included in the total course fee. No extra charges will be applicable.

### Class Schedule

**Q: What are the class timings?**

The classes are scheduled on weekdays as well as weekends, designed with your time and priorities in mind. On weekdays ,classes are scheduled from 8:00 PM onwards and weekend timings are subject to professor’s availability and schedule. The detailed schedule will be shared with you on Day 1 of the program.

**Q: How many hours of engagement is required per week?**

You’ll need to dedicate approximately 8-10 hours per week, which includes attending live sessions, completing tutorials, and engaging in self-paced study to stay on track with the program.

**Q:  How will the class schedule be communicated to students?**

The class schedule will be communicated primarily through the Learning Management System (LMS). It will also be shared on the official WhatsApp community.

**Q: What if I miss a live session, will I be able to access the recording?**

We recommend attending live sessions. In any unforeseen circumstances, if you miss a live class, recordings will be made accessible on the Learning Management System (LMS). Kindly ensure to watch the entire class recording to avoid missing important concepts covered.

**Q: I am a working professional / college student and would like to plan my week ahead. Can I get access to the schedule well in advance?**

Yes, you’ll receive the class schedule and updates every Saturday for the upcoming week, allowing you to plan your time effectively and stay organized.

**Q: Is attendance mandatory for course completion?**

While we strongly recommend attending all live sessions to make the most of the program, in cases where you miss live classes, it’s essential to watch the complete class recordings to stay on track and ensure you grasp the concepts covered. Consistent participation is important for successful course completion and getting the best learning experience.

### Student Support

**Q: How can I raise any queries once I enroll in the program?**

After enrollment, you’ll be onboarded to the Learning Management System (LMS), where you can raise queries via ticketing support or contact your program coordinator for non-conceptual issues.

**Q: How can I interact with my peers?**

Group activities and collaborative projects, provide opportunities to connect and learn from peers.

**Q: Is there a helpline session for student queries?**

Yes, a dedicated live helpline session is available, with details shared in your weekly schedule.

**Q: What learning resources will I learn during the program?**

You’ll have access to notes, pre-reads, class recordings, and assignments on the Learning Management System (LMS).

**Q: How often will assessments be conducted?**

Assessments occur periodically, with evaluations at the end of each module and ongoing case-based work to track progress.

### Placement Opportunities

**Q: Does the program offer placement opportunities?**

If you score 7 CGPA and above and meet the minimum attendance criteria of 65%, which can be achieved through either live sessions or recorded lectures you will be eligible for placement assistance from Masai School, the ed-tech and implementation partner of the program.

**Q: What is the average salary package for graduates of this program?**

Upon completion of the program, students will gain industry-relevant skills and hands-on experience with real-world tools. **Q: What kind of support will I receive to prepare for job interviews?**

If you meet the required performance and engagement criteria during the program, you'll receive dedicated support to get industry-ready.
