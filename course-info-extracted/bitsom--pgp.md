# Frequently Asked Questions

What is this programme about?

The Online PGP in AI-Powered Business Management is a 10-month Executive programme designed for mid and senior professionals from any background. It focuses on building strong business fundamentals while integrating AI across every function.

What is the duration and format of the programme?

The programme runs for approximately 10 months in a blended format. It primarily includes live online classes, supported by self-paced learning, assignments, and a capstone project. In addition, there is a short on-campus immersion at BITSOM focused on leading an AI-enabled transformation.

Is this a technical programme? Do I need coding knowledge?

No, this is a leadership and decision-making programme, not a coding bootcamp and does not require any prior coding knowledge. The emphasis is on understanding AI in a business context, using AI tools to enhance productivity and decision-making, and leading AI-enabled initiatives, rather than building or programming AI systems.

What is campus immersion? Is it mandatory, and when does it take place?

Campus Immersion at BITSoM Powai Optional 5-day in-person experience at BITSoM's Powai campus — network with your cohort, meet faculty face-to-face, and walk the BITS Powai legacy campus

What is the fee for the immersion?

This immersion is non-residential. The applicable immersion fee (if any) will be communicated to participants in advance. The immersion cost is not included in the course fee and will be borne by participants, unless stated otherwise in the programme communication.

What does the immersion include?

The mandatory immersion includes integrating AI across every function(hands-on), faculty interaction, curated academic engagements, and goodies/ memento.
