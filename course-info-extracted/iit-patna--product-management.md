## FAQs

### Program Overview

**Q: What is the Product Management and Agentic AI from Vishlesan i-Hub, IIT Patna about?**

The program is designed to equip learners with the skills to build and manage AI-driven products, focusing on agentic systems. It blends traditional product management with cutting-edge AI technologies like large language models (LLMs) and autonomous agents. Learners explore the product lifecycle, strategic decision-making, and human-AI interaction while working on real-world projects.

**Q: What is the program duration?**

The program spans 6 months, consisting of 2 semesters. Each module includes a project, requiring an 8-10 hour weekly commitment, and the program culminates with a capstone project in the 6th module.

**Q: Will I receive a digital or physical copy of the programme certificate?**

Upon successful completion of the program and fulfillment of all academic requirements, participants will receive a digital copy of the certificate.

**Q: When will I receive my certificate after completing the program?**

Certificates will be issued within 2–3 months from the date of the final examination. The exact timeline for issuance will be communicated during the program.

**Q: What is the mode of delivery for the program?**

All sessions are delivered live online via Zoom.

**Q: Who will be teaching the classes in this program?**

Faculty includes professors from IIMs and IITs, senior product managers from top tech firms, and experienced product managers who conduct weekly doubt-clearing labs.

**Q: What is agentic AI?**

Agentic AI refers to autonomous systems that plan, reason and execute complex tasks end-to-end with minimal human input, acting like intelligent assistants that break goals into subtasks, adapt to change, and interact with tools and APIs to deliver results.

**Q: What makes learners interested in this specific program?**

Many choose the program to pivot into AI-enhanced product roles, gain an IIT-endorsed credential, and build a differentiated portfolio.
