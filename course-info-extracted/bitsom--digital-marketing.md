## FAQs

### Programme Overview

**Q: What is the Programme duration?**

The program spans 6 months, offering a structured and immersive learning journey designed to equip you with in-demand digital marketing skills.

**Q: What topics and concepts are covered in the Programme?**

Professional Certificate in Digital Marketing and Analytics with AI Programme from Bitsom, in collaboration  Masai School, encompasses a comprehensive curriculum covering foundational to advanced topics such as SEO, content and social media marketing, digital analytics, and campaign strategies. Additionally, it integrates generative AI applications to enhance creative content generation, data-driven decision-making, and automation, equipping learners with practical skills for the evolving digital marketing landscape.

**Q: What are the projects I will work on?**

In this course, you will work on practical projects such as creating digital marketing strategies, building customer personas, designing e-commerce product listings, mapping digital customer journeys, and applying the P-O-E-M framework (Paid, Owned, Earned Media).

**Q: Will I receive a digital or physical copy of the programme certificate?**

Upon successful completion of the program and fulfillment of all requirements, participants will receive a digital copy of the certificate. The digital certificate will be dispatched to the registered address shared during enrollment.

**Q: When will I receive my certificate after completing the program?**

Certificates will be issued within 2–3 months from the date of the final examination. The exact timeline for issuance will be communicated during the program.

**Q: What is the mode of delivery for the Programme?**

The mode of delivery of the program is online through the Zoom platform.

**Q: Who will be teaching the classes in this Programme?**

The sessions will be led by esteemed faculty members of BITSoM who bring extensive expertise in digital marketing, consumer behavior, and marketing analytics. In addition to this you will also have sessions led by industry experts, ensuring a comprehensive learning experience. 

**Q: What tools and software will I learn during the course?**

Yes, you will gain hands-on experience with industry-standard tools such as  Jasper, Canva AI, Copy.ai , Surfer SEO, GA4, Meta Ads, HubSpot, Looker Studio & Mailchimp. These tools are essential for executing and analyzing real-world digital marketing campaigns effectively.

**Q: Will I work on real-world case studies?**

Yes, the program includes real-world case studies that enable you to apply your knowledge to practical scenarios, helping you build hands-on experience in solving industry-relevant challenges.

### Eligibility & Admissions

**Q: What are the eligibility criteria for the Programme?**

Anyone with a minimum qualification of 12th grade (or equivalent) and a strong interest in building a career in digital marketing is eligible to apply for the Programme.

**Q: Can international students apply for the Programme?**

Yes, international students can apply for the Programme, provided they meet the eligibility criteria set for the course.

**Q: What is the admission process?**

The admissions process begins with signing up and taking a Qualifier test. If you clear the test, you'll be invited for a one-on-one session where we'll walk you through the program overview and share the next steps to help you complete your enrollment smoothly.

### Fees

**Q: What is the difference between Registration fee and Course fees?**

**Q: How soon will I receive the receipt after paying the registration or course fee?**

A payment acknowledgment will be shared with you within 24–48 business hours of completing the payment.

**Q: Are there any specific financial institutions/partners involved in the EMI process?**

Yes, Masai School, the Ed-tech and implementation partner for this program is associated with trusted NBFCs like Bajaj Finance, ShopSe, Varthana, Propelld, to support you with the EMI process.

**Q: What is the fee payment deadline?**

The fee payment deadline is available on your dashboard. Kindly ensure to complete the fee payment within the timelines to complete your enrolment in the Programme.

**Q: What is the rate of interest on the loan?**

The rate of interest depends upon multiple factors like tenure, amount, credit score, NBFC partner etc. The details will be informed by the finance team for the Programme.

### Class Schedule

**Q: What are the class timings?**

The classes are scheduled on weekdays as well as weekends, designed with your time and priorities in mind. On weekdays ,classes are scheduled from 8:00PM onwards and weekend timings are subject to professor’s availability and schedule. The detailed schedule will be shared with you on Day 1 of the Programme.

**Q: How many hours of engagement is required per week?**

You’ll need to dedicate approximately 6-7 hours per week, which includes attending live sessions, completing tutorials, and engaging in self-paced study to stay on track with the Programme.

**Q: How will the class schedule be communicated to students?**

The class schedule will be communicated primarily through the Learning Management System (LMS). It will also be shared on the official WhatsApp community.

**Q: What if I miss a live session , will I be able to access recording?**

We recommend you to attend Live sessions. In any foreseen circumstances if you miss attending live classes, recordings will be made accessible to you on the Learning Management System (LMS). Kindly ensure to watch the entire class recording to ensure you don't miss important concepts covered.

**Q: I am a working professional / college student and would like to plan my week ahead. Can I get access to the schedule well in advance?**

Yes, you’ll receive the class schedule and updates every Saturday for the upcoming week, allowing you to plan your time effectively and stay organized.

**Q: What is my attendance criteria in the Programme?**

Your attendance eligibility in the Programme is 65%, which can be fulfilled through live sessions or recorded lectures.

**Q: How is attendance calculated?**

Attendance is tracked in two ways:<br><br><strong>Live Attendance</strong> – Counted when you join the live session using your registered email ID and attend at least 80% of the active lecture.<br><br><strong>Recording Attendance</strong> – Counted when you watch the session recording within 30 days of the class <br>This process is automated through the Learning Management System (LMS).

**Q: What happens if the student doesn't meet the required attendance criteria?**

If a student does not meet the required attendance criteria, they will not be eligible to receive the course completion certificate.

**Q: What if I am unable to attend live sessions or watch recordings within 30 days due to some exigencies?**

In such cases, you can raise a support ticket with your respective Programme Coordinator, along with the necessary supporting documents. Exceptions may be granted after reviewing your request.

### Student Support

**Q:  How can I raise any queries once I enroll in the program?**

Once your enrolment is complete, you will be onboarded on Learning Management System (LMS). You can raise your queries through ticketing support and your Programme coordinator support with all non-conceptual queries during the tenure of the course.

**Q: How can I interact with my peers?**

You will have opportunities to engage throughout the program. You can connect with industry mentors and TAs during dedicated doubt-clearing sessions. Additionally, you can interact and collaborate with fellow learners through our learning platforms and private Discord community.

**Q: Is there a helpline session for student queries?**

Yes, a dedicated live helpline session is available to address your queries. Details of the session will be shared as part of your weekly schedule to help you stay informed and supported throughout the Programme.

**Q: What learning resources will be made available during the Programme?**

You will have access to a variety of learning resources, like notes, pre-reads, class recordings, assignments to support learning on Learning Management System (LMS).

**Q: How often will assessments be conducted?**

 Assessments will be conducted periodically throughout the Programme, with evaluations at the end of each module and ongoing project work to track your progress.

**Q: What happens if I don't clear a module?**

You will be given two attempts for evaluation and the best of two scores will be considered for final grading. The minimum clearance criteria is 40%.
