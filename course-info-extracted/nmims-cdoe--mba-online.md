# FAQ's

## Do I need work experience to pursue an MBA (Online) degree?

While work experience is preferred, it is not mandatory to pursue this degree. You can also enroll in the program as a working professional without having to take a break from your job.

## How will an MBA (Online) benefit me in the long run?

The MBA program will certainly help you chart your corporate career at your convenience with an industry-ready curriculum, offering a great work-life-study balance with self-paced learning and flexible timings. It will help you build general management and leadership skills for moving up the corporate ladder.

## Will the session recordings be available?

Yes, session recordings will be available to students once their registration is confirmed and the course has started.

## What is the minimum eligibility for applying?

A candidate must hold a bachelor's degree (10+2+3) from a recognized university with:

• 50% marks for general category applicants
• 45% marks for candidates from OBC/ST/Backward classes
• Work experience is preferred but not mandatory.

## Are there any scholarships available? Who is eligible?

Yes, scholarships are available for students with a defense background or those who have a direct family member in defense services:

• Scholarship benefit: 20% reduction in total program fee
• No other discounts or scholarships are currently available.

## What is the process for applying for an education loan?

Students can apply for a loan through NMIMS's official loan partners at the time of registration:

• Loan/EMI vendors are displayed on the payment portal.
• For specific partners and ROI details, check with your counselor at the time of enrollment.

## What happens if I have a backlog or miss a semester?

The Online MBA is flexible and allows students to complete the program within 4 years:

• You can reappear for missed or backlog subjects in upcoming semesters.
• Additional exam fees may apply for reattempts.
• Your overall degree timeline will be adjusted accordingly.

## How are classes conducted - live or recorded?

The program includes a blend of live interactive lectures and recorded sessions for flexibility:

• Students can attend live sessions as per their chosen track (weekday/weekend).
• Recordings of each lecture are available on the portal for later access.
• Quizzes, assignments, and projects are also hosted digitally.

## What's the difference between an Online MBA and an Offline MBA?

Both deliver a similar curriculum and knowledge, but the Online MBA offers flexible, remote learning while the Offline MBA requires in-person classes and campus attendance.

## What is the refund policy?

In case of admission cancellation, refunds are processed within 10–12 working days as per NMIMS's policy:

• Application fee (₹1,200) is non-refundable.
• Seat confirmation (₹10,000) and other payments are refunded based on the date of cancellation.
