## FAQs

### Program Overview

**Q: What is the Certification Program in Agentic Systems and Design from iHUB DivyaSampark, IIT Roorkee about?**

The Certification Program in Agentic Systems and Design by iHUB DivyaSampark, IIT Roorkee, is a 6-month online course designed to equip professionals from all disciplines with the skills to harness the power of Agentic Systems. The program goes beyond the use of Generative AI tools — it focuses on designing, developing, and deploying intelligent agents capable of planning, reasoning, and acting autonomously to address complex business challenges. Through a hands-on, case study-driven learning approach, participants gain both conceptual insights for leadership roles and practical technical expertise to build real-world AI-driven systems.

**Q: What is the program duration?**

The program duration is 6 months, with a weekly time commitment of 8–10 hours.

**Q: What are the projects I will work on?**

 Throughout the course, learners will work on multiple hands-on projects, culminating in a Capstone Project in Module 5. In this final project, you will identify a real-world business or technical challenge, design and prototype a multi-agent autonomous system to address it, and develop a strategic pitch highlighting ROI, ethical guardrails, and deployment strategy. Earlier modules also include mini-projects such as building a single intelligent agent, testing RAG integration, and designing multi-agent collaboration workflows.

**Q: What topics and concepts are covered in the program?**

The program is divided into five structured modules covering both strategic and technical foundations. These include: Agentic Foundations and Architecture, focusing on planning, reasoning, and action; Agent Components such as Memory, Tools, and RAG; Hands-On Single-Agent Development; Multi-Agent Collaboration and Deployment Strategy; and finally, the Capstone Project, which integrates all learnings into a real-world solution.

**Q: Will I receive a certificate upon completion?**

Yes, upon successful completion of the program, you will receive a Certificate of Completion from iHUB DivyaSampark, IIT Roorkee and Masai, recognizing your achievement.

**Q: What is the mode of delivery for the program?**

The mode of delivery of the program is online through the Zoom platform.

**Q: Who will be teaching the classes in this program?**

Classes are delivered by: professors from IIMs, IITs, senior industry mentors from top tech firms.

**Q: What tools and software will I learn during the course?**

Learners will gain exposure to the most in-demand agentic AI development frameworks and tools, including LangChain, CrewAI, and AutoGen for building and deploying agents. The program also covers RAG (Retrieval-Augmented Generation) frameworks for knowledge grounding, and no-code platforms like n8n, Make.com, and ChatGPT Agents for prototyping workflows. Participants will also develop conceptual understanding of advanced large language models such as OpenAI GPT and Anthropic Claude, which serve as the cognitive engines for agentic systems.

**Q: Will I work on real-world case studies?**

Yes, the program includes real-world case studies that enable you to apply your knowledge to practical scenarios, helping you build hands-on experience in solving industry-relevant challenges.

**Q: What are Agentic Systems?**

Agentic Systems represent the next evolution of AI, moving beyond static, prompt-based models to autonomous systems that can plan, reason, and act. Unlike traditional Generative AI, agentic systems leverage memory, tools, and structured reasoning to perform complex tasks independently. They can function as virtual team members — researchers, analysts, or creative assistants — capable of executing defined goals without constant human supervision.

**Q: What is campus immersion? Is it mandatory and when does it take place?**

The 3-day campus immersion is an optional in-person academic experience designed to enhance learning through faculty interaction and campus exposure. It is typically scheduled towards the end of the program or after completion. While not mandatory, participation is highly recommended. The schedule is shared in advance to enable travel planning.

**Q: What is the fee for the immersion?**

The immersion cost is ₹1,200 + GST per day per participant. This amount is not included in the course fees and must be borne additionally by students.

**Q: What does the immersion include?**

The immersion may include academic sessions and campus interaction opportunities.

**Q: Are accommodation and travel included?**

Limited girls’/female participants accommodation may be available at ₹600 per day (subject to availability). Boys/male participants will need to arrange their accommodation independently. Food is included as part of the immersion. Accommodation and Travel expenses must be borne by students and are not included in the course fees.

**Q: Why should one choose the Certification Program in Agentic Systems and Design?**

The Certification Program in Agentic Systems and Design by iHUB DivyaSampark, IIT Roorkee, uniquely combines product management principles with cutting-edge AI applications. Unlike conventional programs, it emphasizes Agentic AI — empowering learners to design and deploy intelligent systems that can reason, plan, and act autonomously. This 6-month program equips professionals with both strategic and technical expertise to lead AI-driven transformation in their organizations. With a hands-on, case study–driven approach and mentorship from IIT faculty and industry experts, the program helps participants bridge the gap between business innovation and advanced AI implementation.
