## FAQs

### Programme Overview

**Q: What is the program duration?**

The program spans 6 months, offering a structured and immersive learning journey designed to equip you with Product Management skills.

**Q: Will I receive a digital or physical copy of the programme certificate?**

Upon successful completion of the program and fulfillment of all requirements, participants will receive a digital and physical copy of the certificate. The physical certificate will be dispatched to the registered address shared during enrollment.

**Q: When will I receive my certificate after completing the program?**

Certificates will be issued within 2–3 months from the date of the final examination. The exact timeline for issuance will be communicated during the program.

**Q: What is the mode of delivery for the program?**

The mode of delivery of the program is online through the Zoom platform.

**Q: Who will be teaching the classes in this program?**

The program will be taught by esteemed faculty members of IIM Ranchi, including experts in marketing management and product management. Sessions may also include industry experts, providing practical insights and real-world business perspectives.

**Q: What is campus immersion?**

The 3-day campus immersion is an optional in-person immersive experience designed to enhance learning through instructor interaction, campus exposure and networking opportunities.

**Q: What is the fee for the campus immersion?**

The immersion fee varies based on the participation and accommodation option selected: <br/> ₹2,000 + GST per day (Double Occupancy) - includes accommodation and food. <br/>₹3,000 + GST per day (Single Occupancy) - includes accommodation and food. <br/>₹1,000 + GST per day (Non-Residential participation). <br/> Guest house accommodation is subject to availability. The immersion fee is not included in the programme fee and must be paid separately by participants. Travel expenses are not included and must be arranged and borne individually by the participants.

**Q: Is campus immersion mandatory, and when does it take place?**

It is typically scheduled towards the end of the program or after completion. While not mandatory, participation is highly recommended. The schedule will be shared in advance to enable travel planning.

### Eligibility & Admissions

**Q: What are the eligibility criteria for the program?**

Applicants must have a minimum qualification of 12th grade (or equivalent). The program is open to students, working professionals, entrepreneurs, and career starters with an interest in product management and AI.

**Q: Can international students apply for the program?**

Yes, international students are eligible to apply, provided they meet the program's eligibility criteria.

### Fees & Payment

**Q: How soon will I receive the receipt after paying the registration or course fee?**

The receipt will be sent to your registered email address within 24–48 business hours of payment.

**Q: Are there any specific financial institutions/partners involved in the EMI process?**

Yes, EMI options are available through trusted NBFC partners such as Bajaj Finance, ShopSe, Varthana, Propelld, and others.

**Q: What is the fee payment deadline?**

The fee payment deadline will be visible on your student dashboard. Please ensure timely payment to complete your enrollment.

**Q: What is the rate of interest on the loan?**

The interest rate depends on factors such as loan tenure, amount, credit profile, and NBFC partners. The details will be informed by the finance team for the program.

**Q: Is there any additional cost for admission kits?**

The kit cost of ₹600 is already included in the total course fee. There are no additional charges for admission kits.

**Q: Are there any additional charges for assessments during the course?**

The assessment cost of ₹1400, which covers all assessments throughout the course, is already included in the total course fee. No extra charges will be applicable.

### Class Schedule

**Q: What are the class timings?**

The classes are scheduled on weekdays as well as weekends, designed with your time and priorities in mind. On weekdays, classes are scheduled from 8:00PM onwards and weekend timings are subject to professor's availability and schedule. The detailed schedule will be shared with you on Day 1 of the program.

**Q: How many hours of engagement are required per week?**

You'll need to dedicate approximately 8–10 hours per week, which includes attending live sessions, completing tutorials, and engaging in self-paced study to stay on track with the program.

**Q: How will the class schedule be communicated to students?**

The schedule will be shared through the Learning Management System (LMS) and the official WhatsApp community.

**Q: What if I miss a live session—will I be able to access recordings?**

We recommend you to attend Live sessions. In any foreseen circumstances if you miss attending live classes, recordings will be made accessible to you on the Learning Management System (LMS). Kindly ensure to watch the entire class recording to ensure you don't miss important concepts covered.

**Q: Can I get access to the schedule in advance?**

Yes, the weekly schedule is shared every Saturday, allowing you to plan your week effectively.

**Q: Is attendance mandatory for course completion?**

Yes, a minimum of 70% attendance (Live + Recorded) is required to be eligible for course completion and certification.

**Q: How is attendance calculated?**

Attendance is calculated automatically through the Learning Management System (LMS). You will be marked present if you complete either one of the following in full: Live Attendance – Counted when you join the live session in full using your registered email ID and attend the class. Recording Attendance- Counted when you watch the session recording in full within 30 days of the class.

**Q: What happens if the student doesn't meet the required attendance criteria?**

Students who do not meet the attendance requirement will not be eligible for the course completion certificate.

**Q: Will classes be held on weekdays or weekends?**

Live classes are scheduled on weekdays (Monday‑Friday) and Saturdays to balance intensity with flexibility for working professionals and college students. All important sessions start after 8 PM only.

**Q: What if I am unable to attend sessions due to unavoidable circumstances?**

In such cases, you may raise a support ticket with the Program Coordinator along with valid documentation. Requests will be reviewed on a case-by-case basis.

### Student Support & Learning Experience

**Q: How can I raise queries once I enroll in the program?**

After enrollment, you can raise queries via the LMS ticketing system or connect with your Program Coordinator for non-academic concerns.

**Q: How can I interact with my peers?**

You will have opportunities to engage throughout the program. You can connect with industry mentors and TAs during dedicated doubt-clearing sessions. Additionally, you can interact and collaborate with fellow learners through our learning platforms and private Discord community.

**Q: Is there a helpline session for student queries?**

Yes, dedicated live helpline sessions are conducted regularly. Details will be shared in the weekly schedule.

**Q: What learning resources will be made available during the program?**

You will have access to class recordings, reading materials, assignments, case studies, and assessments through the LMS.

**Q: How often will assessments be conducted?**

Assessments are conducted periodically, including module-end evaluations and project-based assessments.

**Q: What happens if I don't clear a module?**

You will be given two attempts, and the best score will be considered. A minimum of 40% is required to clear a module.

### Placement Opportunities

**Q: Does the program offer placement assistance?**

Learners who score above 7 CGPA and meet performance and engagement criteria will be eligible for placement assistance by Masai School, the program's implementation partner.

**Q: What is the average salary package for graduates of this program?**

Salary outcomes depend on factors such as your background, experience, role, and employer. A certification from IIM Ranchi significantly enhances credibility and can improve access to competitive opportunities.

**Q: What kind of support will I receive to prepare for job interviews?**

If you meet the required performance and engagement criteria during the program, you'll receive dedicated support to get industry-ready. This includes resume building, LinkedIn profile enhancement, and mock AI-based interviews—equipping you with the tools and confidence to make the most of available career opportunities.
