Module 01: Getting into the Founder’s Mindset

- • Exploring the entrepreneurial path: transforming ideas into meaningful impact
- • Navigating uncertainty with resilience and strategic risk-taking
- • Strengthening emotional intelligence and inner tenacity
- • Adopting a growth mindset and turning setbacks into learning opportunities
- • Leadership styles for founders: leading with vision and agility

Module 02: Problem-Solving & Idea Discovery

- • Identifying real-world problems worth solving
- • Exploring creative ideation methods like design thinking, SCAMPER, blue ocean strategy etc.
- • Engaging in customer discovery to validate market demand
- • Charting customer pain points, building personas, and mapping user journeys
- • Assessing the viability, growth potential, and appeal of your idea
- • Applying lean startup tactics for fast, feedback-driven iterations

Module 03: Leveraging AI for Smarter Startups

- • Understand the strategic value of AI in the startup ecosystem
- • Explore no-code/low-code AI platforms for rapid prototyping and implementation
- • Learn to automate key business functions to improve efficiency and reduce costs
- • Use AI tools for market research, forecasting, and competitive intelligence
- • Build data-driven, personalized products that enhance customer engagement

Module 04: Prototyping, Building & Product-Market Fit

- • Minimum Viable Product: what, why, and how
- • Wireframing, prototyping tools, and rapid development
- • Running usability tests and gathering early feedback
- • Pivoting or persevering: learning from product iterations

Module 05: Marketing, Traction & Brand Building

- • Defining target audiences and channels for outreach
- • Building a go-to-market strategy (GTM)
- • Leveraging content, social media, and influencer marketing
- • Growth hacking techniques for traction
- • Tracking key performance indicators

Module 06: Business Models, Money & FundRaising

- • Choosing the right business model (freemium, SaaS, marketplace, etc.) and business model innovation techniques
- • Understand unit economics, pricing, and financial viability
- • Build robust financial projections and manage runway effectively
- • Explore the pros and cons of bootstrapping versus external funding
- • Gain insight into the fundraising lifecycle and investor expectations
- • Create compelling pitch decks and master the art of investor communication

Module 07: Scaling, Teams & Demo Day

- • Building high-performance startup teams and culture
- • Hiring for attitude and upskilling for performance
- • Creating scalable systems and processes
- • Managing growth, delegation, and operational complexity
- • Legal essentials: IP, compliance, contracts, and governance
- • Preparing for Demo Day: pitching with clarity and confidence

## FAQs

### Program Overview

**Q: What is the program duration?**

The program spans six months, offering an intensive and structured learning experience on how to build your own venture.

**Q: Will I receive a digital or physical copy of the programme certificate?**

Upon successful completion of the program and fulfillment of all requirements, participants will receive a physical copy of the certificate. The physical certificate will be dispatched to the registered address shared during enrollment.

**Q: When will I receive my certificate after completing the program?**

Certificates will be issued within 2–3 months from the date of the final examination. The exact timeline for issuance will be communicated during the program.

**Q: What is the mode of delivery?**

The program is delivered entirely online through the Zoom platform, allowing you to learn flexibly from anywhere.

**Q: Who will be teaching the classes in this program?**

The sessions will be led by experienced faculty members and Industry Experts who have deep expertise in Entrepreneurship, Leadership, and Innovation. Alongside them, you’ll learn from accomplished Startup Founders, Investors, and Industry Leaders, ensuring a comprehensive and practical learning experience rooted in real-world insights.

**Q: What are the benefits of attending this program?**

This program helps you with a roadmap to transform your idea into a successful startup. You will master business strategy, product development, and innovation, work on real-world projects, and strengthen your leadership skills. Guided by our esteemed faculty and industry experts, you’ll learn how to validate ideas, develop an MVP, and create a pitch deck. The program concludes with Pitch Day, where top teams pitch their startups to investors.

**Q: What is the Master Pitching & Showcase event? Is it mandatory?**

The Master Pitching & Showcase is an exclusive 2-day pitch challenge held at XLRI's campus, where select students are invited based on merit to present their ideas, receive expert feedback, and refine their entrepreneurial or project vision. While participation is not mandatory, it is highly recommended to gain practical exposure and valuable insights.<br/> Please note that any costs for accommodation, food, and travel will be borne by the students and are not included in the course fees.

**Q: What is campus immersion?**

The 2-day campus immersion is an optional in-person immersive experience designed to enhance learning through instructor interaction, campus exposure and networking opportunities. It will also feature a pitch deck presentation, with participation from the Leadership team of XLRI.

**Q: Is campus immersion mandatory and when does it take place?**

It is typically scheduled towards the end of the program or after completion. While not mandatory, participation is highly recommended. The schedule will be shared in advance to enable travel planning.

**Q: What is the fee for the campus immersion?**

No specific immersion fee has been indicated. Any applicable costs will be communicated separately. This fee covers only the arrangements at the immersion venue, including lunch and refreshments provided during the scheduled immersion hours.<br><br>The immersion fee is not included in the programme fee and must be paid separately by participants. Accommodation and travel expenses are not included and must be arranged and borne individually by the participants.

### Eligibility & Admissions

**Q: What are the eligibility criteria for the program?**

This program is designed for Graduates and working professionals.

**Q: Can international students apply for the program?**

Yes, international students can apply for the program, provided they meet the eligibility criteria set for the course.

**Q: What is the admission process?**

Applicants can register by paying the application fees and submit the application form. Upon submission of the application, your profile will undergo a review process. Eligible candidates will then be invited to a one-on-one session to discuss program details and subsequent steps for enrollment.

### Program Fee

**Q: What is the difference between Registration fee and Course fees?**

The Registration Fee secures your seat in the program. The Course Fee is the total program cost, which can be paid upfront or in EMIs with support from our NBFC partner. Your enrollment is confirmed only after the full Course Fee is paid.

**Q: How soon will I receive the receipt after paying the registration or course fee?**

The receipt will be emailed to your registered address within 24–48 business hours of payment.

**Q: Are there any specific financial institutions/partners involved in the EMI process?**

Yes, we’ve partnered with trusted NBFCs like Bajaj Finance, ShopSe, Varthana, Propelld, to support you with the EMI process.

**Q: What is the fee payment deadline?**

The fee payment deadline is available on your dashboard. Kindly ensure to complete the fee payment within the timelines to complete your enrollment in the program.

**Q: What is the rate of interest on the loan?**

The rate of interest depends upon multiple factors like tenure, amount, credit score, NBFC partner etc. The details will be informed by the finance team for the program.

### Class Schedule

**Q: What are the class timings?**

The classes are scheduled on weekdays as well as weekends, designed with your time and priorities in mind. On weekdays, classes are scheduled from 8:00 PM onwards, and weekend timings are subject to the professor’s availability and schedule. The detailed schedule will be shared with you on Day 1 of the program.

**Q: How many hours of engagement are required per week?**

You’ll need to dedicate approximately 6-8 hours per week, which includes attending live sessions, completing tutorials, and engaging in self-paced study to stay on track with the program.

**Q: How will the class schedule be communicated to students?**

The class schedule will be communicated primarily through the Learning Management System (LMS). It will also be shared on the official WhatsApp community.

**Q: What if I miss a live session? Will I be able to access recording?**

We recommend that you attend Live sessions. In any unforeseen circumstances, if you miss attending live classes, recordings will be made accessible to you on the Learning Management System (LMS). Kindly ensure to watch the entire class recording to ensure you don't miss important concepts covered.

**Q: I am a working professional / college student and would like to plan my week. Can I get access to the schedule well in advance?**

Yes, you’ll receive the class schedule and updates every Saturday for the upcoming week, allowing you to plan your time effectively and stay organized.

**Q: Is Attendance mandatory for course completion?**

While we strongly recommend attending all live sessions to make the most of the program, in cases where you miss live classes, it's essential to watch the complete class recordings to stay on track and ensure you grasp the concepts covered. Consistent participation is important for successful course completion and getting the best learning experience.

### Student Support

**Q: How can I raise any queries once I enroll in the program?**

Once your enrolment is complete, you will be onboarded on the Learning Management System (LMS). You can raise your queries through ticketing support and your program coordinator support with all non-conceptual queries during the tenure of the course.

**Q: How can I interact with my peers?**

You will have opportunities to engage throughout the program. You can connect with industry mentors and TAs during dedicated doubt-clearing sessions. Additionally, you can interact and collaborate with fellow learners through our learning platforms and private Discord community.

**Q: Is there a helpline session for student queries?**

Yes, a dedicated live helpline session is available to address your queries. Details of the session will be shared as part of your weekly schedule to help you stay informed and supported throughout the program.

**Q: What learning resources will be made available during the program?**

You will have access to a variety of learning resources, like notes, pre-reads, class recordings, and assignments to support learning on the Learning Management System (LMS).

**Q: How often will assessments be conducted?**

Assessments will be conducted periodically throughout the program, with evaluations at the end of each module and ongoing project work to track your progress.

**Q: What happens if I don't clear a module?**

The program follows a milestone-based approach rather than traditional grading. Progress is evaluated through hands-on deliverables like your MVP one-pager, working prototype, and final product demo. If you're unable to complete a milestone on time, you'll be guided by mentors during weekly sessions to catch up. Active participation, consistent effort, and completing key milestones are essential to successfully completing the program and receiving certification.
