# What Will You Learn?

In this programme, you will learn how to design, execute, and measure end-to-end digital marketing campaigns using data and analytics. You’ll build practical skills across performance marketing, SEO, social media, content, and marketing automation, while learning to read funnels, dashboards, and attribution reports with confidence. You’ll also explore how AI and emerging digital tools are transforming marketing, preparing you to make sharper, data-driven decisions that directly impact growth and ROI.

# 101: Marketing Fundamentals

- Introduction to Marketing- Marketing Mix, STP, Scope and importance
- Conducting Marketing Research and Consumer Insights
- Managing Integrated Marketing Communications
- Managing Digital Products: MVP, Wireframes, Digital Analytics
- Costing and Pricing Fundamentals in Digital Marketing
- PROJECT I: Practice Assignment, Evaluation, and Feedback

# 102: Digital Marketing and Analytics Primer

- Digital Channels Overview: Websites, social media, email, and mobile platforms.
- Customer Journey and Persona Building: Understanding touchpoints and creating customer personas.
- Website Development
- Digital Marketing Fundamentals and Strategies
Scope and comparison with traditional channels, Goal setting, budgeting, and campaign planning.
- Case Studies: Analysis of successful and failed digital marketing campaigns.
- PROJECT II: Practice Assignment, Evaluation, and Feedback

# 103: Content Creation Using AI Tools

- Introduction to AI in Content Marketing: Overview of AI tools for text, visual, and video content.
- Content Ideation and Copywriting: Techniques for generating creative content.
- AI Ethics and Practical Considerations: Responsible use of AI in marketing.
- Landing Page Design
- PROJECT III: Practice Assignment, Evaluation, and Feedback

# 104: Social Media and Influencer Marketing

- Social Media Landscape: Platform analysis and audience segmentation, including platforms like Instagram, LinkedIn, Meta, X (Twitter), Youtube
- Social Media Advertising: Creating, managing, and optimizing paid campaigns; content strategy
- Influencer Marketing: Identifying, onboarding, and managing influencers.
- PROJECT IV: Practice Assignment, Evaluation, and Feedback

# 105: SEO and SEM

- Introduction to SEO: Basics, importance, and trends.
- On-Page and Off-Page SEO: Optimizing content and building backlinks.
- Introduction to SEM: Campaign setup with Google Ads and keyword research; Google AdSense
- Performance Metrics for SEO and SEM: Understanding KPIs and using tools like SEMrush.
- PROJECT V: Practice Assignment, Evaluation, and Feedback

# 106: Data Analytics in Marketing

- Introduction to Marketing Analytics: Importance and role in decision making.
- Google Analytics Basics: Setup, navigation, and reporting.
- Advanced Analytics Tools: Training on Power BI/Tableau/Excel for visualization. Includes CLM tools.
- A/B Testing and Experimentation: Designing and interpreting tests.
- Attribution Models and ROI Calculation: Conversions and marketing ROI.
- Introduction to Campaign Management: Components and lifecycle of a campaign
- PROJECT VI: Practice Assignment, Evaluation, and Feedback

# 107: Capstone Project

- Work in teams on a real/simulated business case, defining target audience, goals, KPIs, and budgets.
- Plan and design an integrated campaign: personas, channel mix, content strategy, and timelines using AI tools.
- Execute the campaign using tools like ChatGPT, Canva, social media, SEO/SEM, automation and Google Analytics.
- Analyse performance, present results and learnings to faculty and peers, with top projects showcased for recognition and career guidance.

## FAQs

### Programme Overview

**Q: What is the programme duration?**

The programme spans 6 months, offering a well-paced and immersive learning experience. It is structured to build your capabilities in digital marketing and analytics through live classes, assignments, and a capstone-style project.

**Q: What topics and concepts are covered in the programme?**

The programme covers marketing fundamentals, digital marketing strategy, Google & Meta Ads, SEO, social media and influencer marketing, email and performance marketing, marketing analytics, dashboards, attribution, A/B testing, and AI tools for content, SEO, and automation. You'll learn how to design and optimize full-funnel digital campaigns.

**Q: What are the projects I will work on?**

You will work on practical, real-world assignments such as creating ad campaigns, optimising landing pages, analysing campaign reports, building dashboards, and using AI tools for content and performance. The programme culminates in an integrated digital marketing project, where you design and present a data-driven campaign for a real or simulated business.

**Q: When will I receive my course completion certificate?**

The course completion certificate will be issued within 60–75 days of successful programme completion. Programme completion refers to the fulfillment of all academic and administrative requirements, including minimum attendance, successful completion of evaluations, and submission of required assignments (if any)

**Q: Will I receive a certificate upon completion?**

Yes. Upon successful completion of the program and fulfillment of all academic requirements, participants will receive a digital copy of the certificate on their registered email Id.

**Q: What will be the criteria to get the certificate?**

A Completion Certificate will be awarded to participants who:

Maintain at least 75% attendance in live sessions, and

Secure a minimum of 50% marks in the overall evaluation (quizzes, assignments, project and/or final assessment).

**Q: When will I receive my certificate after completing the program?**

Certificates will be issued within 2–3 months from the date of the final examination. The exact timeline for issuance will be communicated during the program.

**Q: What is the structure of the programme (online, offline)?**

The programme is delivered online, primarily through live interactive sessions on Zoom, supported by Masai LMS, where you can access resources, assignments, and session recordings.

**Q: Who will be teaching the classes in this programme?**

Classes will be led by IIM Mumbai faculty with expertise in marketing and analytics, along with industry experts working in digital marketing, performance marketing, and growth roles. This ensures a blend of academic rigour and current industry practice.

**Q: What tools and software will I learn during the course?**

You will gain exposure to tools such as Google Analytics, Google Ads, Meta Ads Manager, basic website/landing page builders, Excel, Tableau / Power BI, and AI tools for content creation, SEO optimisation, and marketing automation (e.g., copy and creatives using AI).

**Q: Will I work on real-world case studies?**

Yes. The programme includes real-world and industry-inspired case studies on digital campaigns, social media strategies, SEO/SEM performance, and analytics-led decisions, helping you connect concepts with practical business scenarios.

**Q: Can I apply for the course if I do not have a background in marketing or technology?**

Yes. The programme is suitable for beginners and non-marketers as well. It starts from the basics and gradually moves to advanced topics, as long as you have interest in digital marketing, growth, and data-driven decision-making.

### Eligibility & Admissions

**Q: What are the eligibility criteria for the programme?**

Candidates pursuing an undergraduate degree or higher are eligible to apply. This includes graduates and working professionals from any stream

**Q: Can international students apply for the programme?**

Yes. International learners can apply, provided they meet the eligibility criteria and have the necessary infrastructure (laptop and stable internet) to attend live online sessions.

**Q: What is the admission process?**

The admission process typically consists of:
1. Application / Sign-up for the programme.
2. A Qualifier test to assess basic aptitude.
3. A one-on-one counselling/enrolment call, where the programme details, fee structure, and next steps are explained before you confirm your seat.

**Q: What skills does the Qualifier test assess?**

The Qualifier test checks logical reasoning, numerical/quantitative ability, basic analytical thinking, and communication skills, which support success in a data-driven digital marketing programme.

**Q: Can I take the Qualifier test after my college exams are over?**

Seats are limited, and batches can fill up quickly. You are strongly encouraged to take the Qualifier test at the earliest so that you don't miss the upcoming cohort.

### Fees

**Q: What is the difference between Registration Fee and Course Fee?**

The Registration Fee is a booking amount that secures your seat in the programme. The Course Fee is the total programme fee, payable either in full or through EMI options via Masai’s NBFC partners. Your enrolment is confirmed only after the full Course Fee is paid as per the agreed schedule.

**Q: How soon will I receive the receipt after paying the registration or course fee?**

A payment receipt will be sent to your registered email ID within 24–48 business hours of successful payment.

**Q: Are there any specific financial institutions/partners involved in the EMI process?**

Yes. EMI options are typically facilitated through Masai’s trusted NBFC partners such as Bajaj Finance, ShopSe, Varthana, Propelld, etc. The availability and terms of EMI are subject to the NBFC’s approval.

**Q: What is the fee payment deadline?**

The fee payment deadline and instalment timelines (if applicable) will be clearly mentioned on your dashboard/official communication. You must adhere to these timelines to confirm and retain your seat in the programme.

**Q:  What is the rate of interest on the loan/EMI?**

The rate of interest is determined by the respective NBFC partner and depends on factors such as your profile, loan amount, and tenure. Detailed information will be shared by the finance/loan team during the EMI process.

**Q: Are the programme fees refundable?**

All programme fees are non-refundable. Candidates are advised to carefully review programme details and clarify any queries before confirming their enrolment.

**Q: Is there any financial aid or scholarship available?**

Yes, scholarships are offered based on your performance in the Qualifier test. The programme reserves the right to determine the eligibility and extent of the scholarship awarded to each candidate.

### Class Schedule

**Q: What are the class timings?**

Classes are scheduled keeping the needs of working professionals and students in mind. Participants can expect 3 hours of live, faculty-led sessions every week, conducted on Saturdays from 5:00 PM to 8:00 PM. Please note that minor changes to the schedule may be possible based on faculty availability.

**Q:  How many hours of engagement are required per week?**

You should plan for about 6–7 hours per week, which includes 3 hours of live classes, self-paced learning, tutorials, and project work.

**Q: How will the class schedule be communicated to students?**

The schedule and updates will be shared via the Masai’ Learning Management System (LMS) and also through official channels such as WhatsApp / email, so you are always informed in advance.

**Q: What if I miss a live session – will I be able to access the recording?**

Yes. While we strongly recommend attending live sessions for the best learning experience, recordings of classes will be available on the LMS for about 1 week (7 days) from the date of the session so you can catch up. Please note that watching recordings does not count towards attendance.

**Q: I am a working professional / college student and would like to plan my week ahead. Can I get access to the schedule well in advance?**

Yes. The weekly schedule and updates are shared in advance (typically over the weekend) so that you can plan your work, classes, and personal commitments accordingly.

**Q: Is attendance mandatory for course completion?**

 Yes. To be eligible for the Completion Certificate, you must maintain at least 75% attendance in live sessions, in addition to meeting the minimum performance criteria in assessments.

**Q: For how long will the session recordings be accessible after a live class?**

The recordings of each live session will be available on the LMS for up to 7 days from the date of the lecture (T+7 days). Please note that viewing the session recording does not count towards attendance. Attendance is marked only for participation in the live class.

### Student Support

**Q: How can I raise any queries once I enroll in the programme?**

After enrolment, you will be onboarded onto the Masai LMS, where you can raise queries through a ticketing/support system. A dedicated programme coordinator will also assist you with non-academic queries throughout the course.

**Q: How can I interact with my peers?**

You will participate in group activities and projects, and may also be part of official WhatsApp/Slack communities where you can discuss ideas, collaborate on assignments, and build a strong peer network.

**Q:  Is there a helpline session for student queries?**

Yes. There will be dedicated doubt-clearing/helpline sessions scheduled during the programme to address both academic and process-related questions.

**Q: What learning resources will be made available during the programme?**

 You will have access to lecture notes, class recordings (for 1 week per session), assignments, reference material, and case studies via the LMS to support your learning and revision.

**Q: How often will assessments be conducted?**

Assessments are conducted periodically throughout the programme, including module-wise quizzes, assignments, practical tasks, and a final/capstone project to track your progress and readiness.

**Q: Will I be able to interact with the faculty in the live class ?**

The students may interact with the faculty during the dedicated Q&A segment at the end of each live class. They can ask their questions, seek clarifications, and get your doubts resolved during that time.

### Campus Immersion

**Q: What is campus immersion? Is it mandatory, and when does it take place?**

The 2-day campus immersion is an optional in-person academic experience designed to enhance learning through faculty interaction and campus exposure. It is typically scheduled towards the end of the program or after completion. While not mandatory, participation is highly recommended. The schedule is shared in advance to enable travel planning.

**Q: Is there an immersion fee?**

There is no additional immersion fee. However, related personal expenses are not included in the course fees and must be borne by students.

**Q: What does the immersion include?**

The immersion may include faculty-led sessions, program-related activities, and campus tours.

**Q: Are accommodation and travel included?**

Food is included. Accommodation and Travel expenses must be borne by students and are not included in the course fees.
