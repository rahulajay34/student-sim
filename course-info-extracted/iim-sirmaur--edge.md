# What Will You Learn?

This program builds a strong foundation in analytics, Generative AI, automation, and business statistics, with practical applications across HR, marketing, and operations analytics. You’ll explore AI fairness, text analytics, clustering, predictive modelling, consumer analytics, forecasting, and sentiment analysis, while learning to design interactive dashboards and automation workflows using tools like Power BI, Orange, PSPP, Python, Seaborn, and Matplotlib. The program combines theory with hands-on projects and a final capstone, enabling you to apply AI-driven insights and automation to real-world business problems.

## FAQs

### Program Overview

**Q: What is the program duration?**

The program spans 6 months, offering a structured and immersive learning experience designed to equip participants with in-demand skills in analytics, Generative AI, and domain-specific business applications.

**Q: Will I receive a digital or physical copy of the programme certificate?**

Upon successful completion of the program and fulfillment of all requirements, participants will receive a physical copy of the certificate. The physical certificate will be dispatched to the registered address shared during enrollment.

**Q: When will I receive my certificate after completing the program?**

Certificates will be issued within 2–3 months from the date of the final examination. The exact timeline for issuance will be communicated during the program.

**Q: What is the mode of delivery for the program?**

The mode of delivery of the program is online through the Zoom platform.

**Q: Who will be teaching the classes in this program?**

The sessions will be led by expert faculty from IIM Sirmaur and supported by industry practitioners. You'll learn directly from Data & GenAI specialists with deep experience in both corporate and academic domains.

**Q: What is campus immersion?**

The 3-day campus immersion is an optional in-person immersive experience designed to enhance learning through instructor interaction, campus exposure and networking opportunities. It will also feature a pitch deck presentation, with participation from the Leadership team of IIM Sirmaur.

**Q: Is campus immersion mandatory, and when does it take place?**

It is typically scheduled towards the end of the program or after completion. While not mandatory, participation is highly recommended. The schedule will be shared in advance to enable travel planning.

**Q: What is the fee for the campus immersion?**

The campus immersion fee is ₹2,500 to ₹4,000 + GST per participant, per day. This fee covers only the arrangements at the immersion venue, including lunch and refreshments provided during the scheduled immersion hours.<br><br>The immersion fee is not included in the programme fee and must be paid separately by participants. Accommodation and travel expenses are not included and must be arranged and borne individually by the participants.

### Eligibility & Admissions

**Q: What are the eligibility criteria for the program?**

The program is open to beginners. No prior experience in Data or GenAI is required, making it suitable for students, early professionals, and individuals from both tech and non-tech backgrounds.

**Q: Can international students apply for the program?**

Yes, international students can apply for the program, provided they meet the eligibility criteria set for the course.

**Q: What is the admission process?**

The admissions process begins with signing up and taking a Qualifier test. If you clear the test, you'll be invited for a one-on-one session where we'll walk you through the program overview and share the next steps to help you complete your enrollment smoothly.

**Q: What skills does the Qualifier test assess?**

The Qualifier test evaluates your logical reasoning, data interpretation, quantitative aptitude, and communication skills, which are basic requirements of the program.

**Q: Can I take the Qualifier test after my college exams are over?**

Seats are limited, so we strongly recommend taking the Qualifier test at the earliest to avoid missing the opportunity to kickstart your career in Data Analytics.

### Fees

**Q: What is the difference between the Registration fee and Course fees?**

The Registration Fee secures your seat in the program. The Course Fee is the total program cost, which can be paid upfront or in EMIs with support from our NBFC partner. Your enrollment is confirmed only after the full Course Fee is paid.

**Q: How soon will I receive the receipt after paying the registration or course fee?**

The receipt will be emailed to your registered address within 24–48 business hours of payment.

**Q: Are there any specific financial institutions/partners involved in the EMI process?**

Yes, we've partnered with trusted NBFCs like Bajaj Finance, ShopSe, Varthana, Propelld, to support you with the EMI process.

**Q: What is the fee payment deadline?**

The fee payment deadline is available on your dashboard. Kindly ensure to complete the fee payment within the timelines to complete your enrolment in the program.

**Q: What is the rate of interest on the loan?**

The rate of interest depends upon multiple factors like tenure, amount, credit score, NBFC partner etc. The details will be informed by the finance team for the program.

### Class Schedule

**Q: What are the class timings?**

Classes run on weekdays and weekends. Weekday classes start from 8:00 PM onwards, while weekend timings depend on faculty availability. The detailed schedule is shared on Day 1 of the program.

**Q: How many hours of engagement is required per week?**

You'll need to dedicate approximately 8-10 hours per week, which includes attending live sessions, completing tutorials, and engaging in self-paced study to stay on track with the program.

**Q: How will the class schedule be communicated to students?**

The class schedule will be communicated primarily through the Learning Management System (LMS). It will also be shared on the official WhatsApp community.

**Q: What if I miss a live session, will I be able to access the recording?**

We recommend you to attend Live sessions. In any unforeseen circumstances, if you miss attending live classes, recordings will be made accessible to you on the Learning Management System (LMS). Kindly ensure to watch the entire class recording to ensure you don't miss important concepts covered.

**Q: I am a working professional / college student and would like to plan my week ahead. Can I get access to the schedule well in advance?**

Yes, you'll receive the class schedule and updates every Saturday for the upcoming week, allowing you to plan your time effectively and stay organized.

**Q: Is there any attendance criteria in this program for course completion?**

Yes, to be eligible for course completion, you must maintain a minimum of 75% attendance, which can be achieved through either live sessions or recorded lectures.

**Q: How is attendance calculated?**

Attendance is calculated automatically through the Learning Management System (LMS). You will be marked present if you complete either one of the following in full:<br><br><strong>Live Attendance</strong> – Counted when you join the live session in full using your registered email ID and attend the class in full.<br><br><strong>Recording Attendance</strong> – Counted when you watch the session recording in full within 30 days of the class.

**Q: What happens if the student doesn’t meet the required attendance criteria?**

If a student does not meet the required attendance criteria, they will not be eligible to receive the course completion certificate.

**Q: What if I am unable to attend live sessions or watch recordings within 30 days due to some exigencies?**

In such cases, you can raise a support ticket with your respective Program Coordinator, along with the necessary supporting documents. Exceptions may be granted after reviewing your request.

### Student Support

**Q: How can I raise any queries once I enroll in the program?**

Once your enrolment is complete, you will be onboarded on the Learning Management System (LMS). You can raise your queries through ticketing support and your program coordinator support with all non-conceptual queries during the tenure of the course.

**Q: How can I interact with my peers?**

You will have opportunities to engage throughout the program. You can connect with industry mentors and TAs during dedicated doubt-clearing sessions. Additionally, you can interact and collaborate with fellow learners through our learning platforms and private Discord community.

**Q: Is there a helpline session for student queries?**

Yes, a dedicated live helpline session is available to address your queries. Details of the session will be shared as part of your weekly schedule to help you stay informed and supported throughout the program.

**Q: What learning resources will be made available during the program?**

You will have access to a variety of learning resources, like notes, pre-reads, class recordings, and assignments to support learning on the Learning Management System (LMS).

**Q: How often will assessments be conducted?**

Assessments will be conducted periodically throughout the program, with evaluations at the end of each module and ongoing project work to track your progress.

**Q: What happens if I don't clear a module?**

You get two attempts per evaluation; the higher score counts. A minimum of 40 % is required to pass a module.

Yes. Learners who score 7 CGPA or above and maintain a minimum of 75% attendance throughout the program will be eligible for placement assistance provided by Masai School, the ed-tech and implementation partner for this program.

Hence, there is no fixed or guaranteed average salary associated with the program.

Learners who meet the required performance and engagement criteria will receive structured career support aimed at improving industry readiness.
