Module 1: Supply Chain Dynamics

- • Industry trends affecting change
- • Role of Data, Analytics, and Automation in business performance
- • Data Science for new business models and improved operations

Module 2: Supply Chain Dynamics (Continued)

- • Customer value proposition and operations strategy
- • The Bullwhip Effect and strategies to control it
- • The value of information in supply chains
- • Case Studies: PC Manufacturer, CPG Network Optimization, Fashion Retailer

Module 3: Supply Chain Integration

- • Push-Pull Strategies
- • Framework for matching supply and demand
- • Reducing complexity in supply chains
- • Supply Chain Digitization
- • Unified View of Demand

Module 4: Supply Chain Digitization

- • Integrating internal and external data
- • Effective demand forecasting process
- • Supply Chain Segmentation
- • Smart S&OP, Smart Execution, Control Tower
- • Roadmap for supply chain digitization

Module 5: Supply Chain Resiliency, Redundancy, and Flexibility

- • Risk Exposure Index for resiliency
- • Stress Testing: Time to Recover (TTR) and Time to Survive (TTS)
- • Flexibility’s impact on inventory management
- • Case Studies: Ford, PepsiCo, High Tech, COVID-19, Russia-Ukraine War

Module 6: Supply Chain Sustainability

- • Sustainability as a competitive advantage
- • Trade-offs between efficiency, responsiveness, and carbon emissions
- • Opportunities to reduce carbon emissions
- • Case Studies: CPG Network Design, Waste Reduction

## FAQs

### Program Overview

**Q: What is the program duration?**

The program spans 6 weeks, delivering a focused and intensive learning experience to equip you with essential skills in smart supply chain digitization.

**Q: What topics and concepts are covered in the program?**

The program covers a comprehensive curriculum across six weeks, including supply chain dynamics (industry trends, bullwhip effect, data analytics, automation), integration (push-pull strategies, demand matching), digitization (digital twins, demand forecasting, smart S&OP), resiliency (risk exposure, flexibility, stress testing), and sustainability (carbon emissions, waste reduction). It equips learners with skills to optimize modern supply chains using data-driven strategies.

**Q: Will I receive a certificate upon completion?**

Yes, upon successful completion of the program, you will receive a certificate from IIM Mumbai. This certification is awarded based on your overall performance and fulfillment of the program’s completion criteria. IIM Mumbai upholds a strong standard of academic rigor. To be eligible for the certificate, participants must maintain a minimum of 75% attendance and complete at least 3 out of the 5 quizzes.

**Q: What are the attendance criteria?**

Participants must attend at least 75% of the session duration to be marked as present.

**Q: What is the mode of delivery for the program?**

The mode of delivery of the program is online through the Webex.

**Q: Who will be teaching the classes in this program?**

The sessions will be led by Prof. David Simchi-Levi MIT, USA and Prof. Manoj K. Tiwari, IIM Mumbai faculty members of IIM Mumbai, renowned for their expertise in supply chain management, analytics, and digitization, alongside industry experts who bring practical insights from leading organizations, ensuring a comprehensive learning experience.

**Q: What tools and software will I learn during the course?**

You will gain exposure to tools such as advanced Excel and a few other tools based on toopics for data analysis, demand forecasting, and modeling. These tools are critical for optimizing and analyzing data-driven supply chain operations effectively.

**Q: Will I work on real-world case studies?**

Yes, the program includes real-world case studies discussed in class, such as PC manufacturing, CPG network optimization, Ford's supply chain resiliency, and PepsiCo's disruption management, allowing you to apply supply chain digitization, resiliency, and sustainability concepts to address industry-relevant challenges.

**Q: Is there a minimum criterion to receive a certificate?**

Yes, to receive a certificate of completion, a student must have at least 75% attendance and attempt at least 3 out of the 5 quizzes.

### Eligibility & Admissions

**Q: What are the eligibility criteria for the program?**

Anyone with a minimum qualification of 12th grade (or equivalent) and a strong interest in building a career in supply chain management or digitization is eligible to apply for the program. This program is Ideal for students, early-career professionals looking to build expertise in supply chain principles, and lead transformation in modern supply chain operations. (Knowledge of basic Analytics will be helpful)

**Q: Can international students apply for the program?**

Yes, international students can apply for the program, provided they meet the eligibility criteria set for the course.

### Class Schedule

**Q: What are the class timings?**

The classes are scheduled on Saturdays and Sundays from 5:00 PM to 7:30 PM IST, designed to accommodate your time and priorities. The detailed schedule will be shared with you on Day 1 of the program.

**Q: How many hours of engagement is required per week?**

You'll need to dedicate approximately 6-7 hours per week, which includes attending live sessions of 5 hours and Self study.

**Q: How will the class schedule be communicated to students?**

The class schedule will be communicated primarily through email.

**Q: What if I miss a live session, will I be able to access recording?**

We recommend you attend live sessions. In any unforeseen circumstances, if you miss attending live classes, recordings will be made accessible to you for a pre-defined time as per Institute guidelines. Kindly ensure to watch the entire class recording to ensure you don't miss important concepts covered.

**Q: I am a working professional / college student and would like to plan my week ahead. Can I get access to the schedule well in advance?**

Yes, you'll receive the class schedule and updates well in advance, allowing you to plan your time effectively and stay organized.

**Q: What is my attendance criteria in the program?**

Your attendance eligibility in the program is 75%, which can be fulfilled through live sessions.
