# What Will You Learn?

This 6-month online certification is designed for mid-level managers, aspiring leaders, and experienced professionals seeking to lead effectively in the AI era. The programme provides a strategic, non-technical understanding of AI, enabling participants to identify business opportunities, evaluate AI solutions, and guide teams through transformation. Through a practical, case-based learning approach and real-world examples across healthcare, telecom, retail, manufacturing, and other sectors, the course highlights how AI can drive innovation, efficiency, and sustainable growth.

# Module 1: AI Foundations for Leaders

- Understanding the AI Landscape: High-level overview of key AI concepts like Machine Learning (predictive models), Deep Learning, Generative AI, and Agentic AI – when to use what AI type, and Data Science Life cycle
- The Business Value of AI: Identifying and articulating the potential for AI to drive revenue, reduce costs, and improve customer experience – How do you measure the productivity gain and AI adoption across the organization
- Data as a Strategic Asset: How to assess the quality and availability of data needed for a successful ML project and the importance of data collection as an investment; Data driven decision making; Data strategy – data warehouse, data lake, data mesh, data lakehouse – when to use what strategy?
- Case Study Exploration: An analysis of foundational AI success stories and failures in various industries, focusing on the strategic decisions that led to their outcomes

# Module 2: Applied AI in Business Decisions

- Supervised Learning for Prediction: Introduction to Regression (predicting sales or stock prices) and Classification (predicting customer churn or fraud) models. Computer vision (classification, object detection and segmentation) aspects.
- Unsupervised Learning for Insights: Understanding how Clustering and Anomaly Detection models can be used to uncover patterns in data, such as customer segmentation or identifying unusual network activity. Semi-supervised approaches
- Reinforcement Learning concept with case studies: How RL works and when to use RL, Understanding the concepts of agent, environment, states, reward, penalty mechanism and learning policy
- Recommendation Systems: Covering data filtering techniques such as collaborative filtering, content based and hybrid approaches
- Evaluating Model Performance: Understanding key performance metrics (e.g., accuracy, precision, recall), Monitoring and Observability beyond evaluation
- Practical AI use cases covering Supervised, unsupervised, reinforcement learning: Telecom – anomaly detection, churn prediction (sales), Traffic balancing classification use cases; Healthcare – bone age prediction, image classification, detection and segmentation example; Personalized recommendations in FinTech, e-commerce and Learning & Development; AI for supply chain – demand and supply forecasting (predictions), warehouse automation (autonomous cars, RL based path planning)

# Module 3: Leading AI Initiatives: Strategy, Ethics, and Governance

- Building an AI Roadmap: Creating a strategic plan for AI implementation, prioritizing projects based on business impact, and allocating resources effectively, MLOps, AI maturity of the organization, Path to become AI-first organization, Build vs Buy decision making and Scale to RoI
- Navigating the Human-AI Teaming Model: Strategies for training and motivating your workforce to collaborate effectively with AI tools, specifically addressing the rise of Generative and Agentic AI
- Ethical AI and Responsible Use: Understanding the ethical considerations of AI, including bias, privacy, and transparency, and how to establish a governance framework for responsible AI use, excerpts from GDPR act, DPDP Act, EU AI Act for example, Explainable AI
- Change Management and Communication: How to champion and lead AI initiatives, overcome resistance, and foster a data-driven culture by communicating the "why" behind the change
- Practical case study of driving Responsible AI in an organization

# Module 4: Campus Immersion – Gen AI and Agentic AI

- Gen AI concepts and use cases: Transformers, GPT, LLMs, Prompt Engineering, RAG, Fine Tuning, LLM Observability
- Strategic MLOps and LLMOps
- Agentic workflows and multi-agent systems
- Enterprise AI: Document and database search using Gen AI; challenges and strategic decisions
- Build vs Buy strategic decision and scale to RoI
- Building AI agents (practice session)

# Module 5: Capstone Project: Bringing It All Together

- Problem Identification: Defining a clear, high-impact problem to solve with AI
- Solution Design: Outlining an AI strategy and implementation plan
- Business Case Creation: Developing a compelling presentation to gain buy-in and resources for your project

## FAQs

### Programme Overview

**Q: What is this programme about?**

This is a 6-month executive programme designed to help mid-career professionals and leaders understand, evaluate, and lead AI initiatives without needing to code. You will learn how to identify high-impact AI opportunities, make confident data-informed decisions, and drive AI-led change in your organisation through real-world case studies and a capstone project.

**Q: What is the duration and format of the programme?**

The programme runs for approximately 6 months in a blended format. It primarily includes live online classes, supported by self-paced learning, assignments, and a capstone project. In addition, there is a short on-campus immersion at BITSOM focused on Generative AI and Agentic AI.

**Q: Is this a technical programme? Do I need coding knowledge?**

No, this is a leadership and decision-making programme, not a coding bootcamp. The focus is on understanding AI concepts, use-cases, strategy, governance, and business impact from a leader's perspective. You do not need prior coding or data science experience.

**Q: Who will be teaching the classes in this programme?**

Sessions will be led by BITSOM faculty members with expertise in AI, analytics, strategy, and leadership, complemented by senior industry practitioners who bring practical perspectives on AI adoption, digital transformation, and leadership in modern organisations.

**Q: What is campus immersion? Is it mandatory, and when does it take place?**

The campus immersion is a mandatory, in-person academic experience conducted at BITSoM’s Powai campus. It is typically scheduled towards the end of the programme or shortly after the programme concludes.

**Q: What is the fee for the immersion?**

This immersion is non-residential. The applicable immersion fee (if any) will be communicated to participants in advance. The immersion cost is not included in the course fee and will be borne by participants, unless stated otherwise in the programme communication.

**Q: What does the immersion include?**

The mandatory immersion includes AI agent-building (hands-on), faculty interaction, curated academic engagements, and goodies/memento.

**Q: Are accommodation, food, and travel included?**

As this is a non-residential immersion, accommodation is not included. Travel costs are also to be borne by participants and are not included in the course fee.

**Q: When will the detailed immersion schedule be shared?**

The detailed agenda and logistics will be shared closer to the immersion dates once the schedule is finalized.

**Q: Will I receive a certificate upon completion?**

Yes. Upon successful completion of the program and fulfilment of all academic requirements, participants will receive a digital copy of the certificate. The digital certificate will be dispatched to the registered email address shared during enrollment.

**Q: When will I receive my certificate after completing the program?**

Certificates will be issued within 2–3 months from the date of the final examination. The exact timeline for issuance will be communicated during the program.

**Q: How will I be evaluated?**

Your performance will be evaluated through periodic assessments, module tests, class participation, and a final capstone project. Module tests are typically conducted after key modules, and the capstone project is mandatory for successful completion. Detailed evaluation criteria, including minimum passing requirements, will be shared at the start of the programme.

### Eligibility & Admissions

**Q: Who is this programme best suited for?**

This programme is ideal for mid-career professionals, managers, and leaders with 3-10 years of professional experience who want to use AI to make better business decisions, lead AI-enabled projects, and stay relevant in an AI-driven world. It is suitable for professionals from tech, product, strategy, consulting, operations, finance, HR, marketing, and other business functions.

**Q: What are the eligibility criteria?**

A minimum of 3-10 years of professional work experience post graduation is required. Professionals from all industries and functional backgrounds are welcome to apply. There is no requirement for prior coding or data science knowledge.

**Q: Can international professionals apply for the programme?**

Yes, international professionals can apply, provided they meet the work experience and other eligibility criteria. Class timings and participation requirements will be communicated in IST (Indian Standard Time).

**Q: What is the admission process?**

The admission process typically includes:<br><br>1. Submitting an online application form with your professional details.<br>2. Profile review by the admissions team to assess fit and motivation.<br>3. A short interaction or consultation (if required) to clarify expectations and help you make an informed decision.<br><br>Detailed steps and timelines will be shared on the application page.

**Q: Is there any entrance or qualifier test?**

This is a leadership-oriented programme, so there is no coding or technical entrance test. Selection is primarily based on your profile, experience, and alignment with the learning objectives. If any additional assessment is introduced, it will be clearly communicated during the admissions process.

### Fees & Payment

**Q: What is the programme fee?**

The total programme fee and applicable taxes will be INR 1.5 Lakhs + Taxes. Any additional costs related to travel, accommodation, or personal expenses for the campus immersion are to be borne by participants.

**Q: What is the difference between the registration fee and programme fee?**

The registration fee is a non-refundable amount paid to block your seat in the cohort. The programme fee is the total cost of the course, which can typically be paid upfront or in instalments, as per the payment plans available.

**Q: Are EMI or loan options available?**

Yes, EMI/loan facilities may be available through Masai's NBFC partners to help you spread out the fee payment over a period of time. Details of available partners, interest rates, and documentation requirements will be shared by the programme's finance or admissions team at the time of enrolment.

**Q: What is the fee payment deadline?**

The fee payment deadline and instalment schedule (if applicable) will be visible on your dashboard. Please ensure that payments are completed within the specified timelines to confirm and retain your seat in the programme.

### Class Schedule & Learning Format

**Q: What are the class timings?**

Live online classes are scheduled to be convenient for working professionals, typically during evenings or over weekends. Exact days and timings will be shared in advance before the batch starts.

**Q: How many hours of engagement are required per week?**

You should plan for around 8–10 hours per week, which includes live classes, self-paced learning, reading or pre-class work, and assignments or project activities.

**Q: How will the class schedule be communicated?**

The schedule will be shared through the Learning Management System (LMS) and official communication channels such as email or WhatsApp groups. Any changes or updates will be notified well in advance.

**Q: What if I miss a live session? Will recordings be available?**

While live participation is strongly encouraged for discussions and Q&A, session recordings will be made available on the LMS for you to catch up in case you miss a class. You are expected to go through the recordings to stay aligned with the cohort.

**Q: Is attendance mandatory for course completion?**

Yes, maintaining a 65% attendance is mandatory as per programme policy, since this is a high-engagement leadership programme.

**Q: What is the certification criteria?**

To be eligible for the certificate, you are required to maintain a minimum of 65% attendance, including the time spent watching recorded sessions. You must also achieve a minimum passing percentage of 40%.

### Learning Experience & Support

**Q: What role does Masai play in this programme?**

Masai is the implementation and learning support partner. Masai provides the LMS platform, manages programme operations, coordinates schedules, and offers learner support and doubt-resolution, so that you have a seamless experience throughout the programme.

**Q: How can I raise queries or doubts once I enrol?**

Once enrolled, you will be onboarded to the LMS and official communication channels. You can raise queries via the LMS ticketing or support system, your programme coordinator for non-academic issues, and designated doubt-clearing or helpline sessions for academic queries.

**Q: How will I interact with peers?**

You'll collaborate with peers through group discussions, breakout activities, and especially during group assignments and capstone work. The on-campus immersion also offers valuable face-to-face networking with fellow participants, faculty, and industry guests.

**Q: What kind of learning resources will I get?**

You will receive curated reading material, pre-reads, lecture slides, case studies, class recordings, and assignment guidelines via the LMS. Additional resources may be shared by faculty for deeper exploration.

**Q: How often will assessments be conducted?**

Assessments will be conducted periodically through quizzes, module tests, reflective assignments, and the final capstone project. These are designed to reinforce learning and contribute to your overall completion status.

### Career Advancement & Outcomes

**Q: Does this programme offer job placement support?**

No, this programme is not a job-placement or career-switch bootcamp. It is an executive-level leadership programme focused on upskilling and career advancement for working professionals. The primary outcome is your ability to lead AI-driven initiatives and make better strategic decisions in your current or future roles, not entry-level job placement.

**Q: How will this programme help my career?**

You will gain the language, frameworks, and confidence to identify and prioritise AI use-cases, evaluate AI projects and vendors more effectively, lead cross-functional teams on AI initiatives, and champion responsible and ethical AI adoption. Combined with the BITSOM executive credential and alumni network, this can significantly strengthen your leadership profile and internal or external career prospects.

**Q: Will there be opportunities for networking?**

Yes. You will interact closely with a cohort of experienced professionals during group work, and the campus immersion. In addition, eligible participants may gain access to BITSOM's executive alumni network, which can be valuable for long-term professional connections.

**Q: Will I build any tangible output I can showcase?**

Yes. Through the capstone project, you will work on a real-world problem from your industry or organisation, design an AI-enabled solution, and prepare a board-style business case. This becomes a tangible artefact you can discuss with your leadership, stakeholders, or in future professional conversations.
