## FAQs

### Program Overview

**Q: What is the AI, Analytics, and Disruptive Technologies for Business Intelligence program?**

The AI, Analytics, and Disruptive Technologies for Business Intelligence programme is a 6-week intensive online offering by IIM Mumbai designed to prepare learners to lead and manage business transformation in an increasingly uncertain and technology-driven environment. The program focuses on how analytics, artificial intelligence, blockchain, and other emerging technologies can be strategically embedded into business decision-making. Through a combination of live faculty-led sessions, enterprise simulations, and real-world case discussions, participants gain a practical understanding of how modern organizations leverage data and disruptive technologies to drive sustainable competitive advantage.

**Q: What is the program duration?**

The programme is conducted over a period of six weeks and is designed to deliver a focused learning experience within a short, intensive timeframe.

**Q: How is the programme structured?**

The programme is divided into two distinct phases. The first phase, spanning Weeks 1 to 4, focuses on building a strong strategic leadership foundation, covering analytics-driven decision-making, uncertainty management, and disruptive technologies. The second phase, conducted during Weeks 5 and 6, emphasizes advanced implementation, where strategic concepts are translated into enterprise-scale operational execution through applied frameworks and simulations.

**Q: How many sessions are included in the programme?**

The programme consists of a total of twelve live sessions, conducted at a pace of two sessions per week. Each session is designed to balance conceptual understanding with applied learning.

**Q: What is the expected weekly time commitment?**

Participants are expected to dedicate approximately 5 hours per week to live classes, with each session running for about two and a half hours. In addition to the live sessions, participants may spend some time on reflection, preparation, and engagement with the learning material as required.

### Eligibility & Admissions

**Q: Who is eligible to apply for this program?**

The programme is suitable for undergraduate and postgraduate students, early- and mid-career professionals, managers, entrepreneurs, and consultants who are interested in building strategic capability in analytics, AI, and disruptive technologies. A strong interest in business decision-making and digital transformation is recommended.

**Q: Is prior experience in analytics or AI mandatory?**

Prior technical experience is not mandatory. The programme is structured to build conceptual clarity and strategic understanding, making it accessible to participants from diverse academic and professional backgrounds.

### Student Support

**Q: How will program-related communication and learning resources be shared?**

All official communication, session links, and any learning resources relevant to the program will be shared through designated communication channels during the course duration.

**Q: How will programme-related communication and learning resources be shared?**

All official communication, including session links and any learning resources relevant to the program, will be shared with participants through email on their registered email addresses during the course duration.

### Curriculum & Learning Experience

**Q: What topics and concepts are covered in the programme?**

The programme covers a comprehensive range of topics related to business intelligence and digital transformation. These include managing uncertainty using business analytics, understanding information distortion through the Bullwhip Effect, evaluating the business value of AI, Generative AI, and Agentic AI, and exploring emerging technologies such as blockchain, IoT, and Industry 5.0. Participants also learn about demand forecasting, digital control towers, digital twins, omnichannel commerce, and strategic frameworks required to lead organizations through continuous disruption.

**Q: Does the program include hands-on learning?**

The programme incorporates experiential elements through faculty-led simulations and case study discussions designed to illustrate real-world business scenarios. These sessions help participants develop strategic perspective and decision-making clarity by observing and analyzing outcomes under uncertainty and complexity, rather than through direct tool-based execution.

**Q: Will real-world case studies be discussed during the programme?**

Yes, real-world case studies form an integral part of the programme and will be discussed during the sessions. These case discussions are led by the faculty to illustrate how organizations approach digital transformation and analytics-driven decision-making in evolving business environments, helping participants build strategic understanding and contextual clarity.

**Q: Is the programme suitable for participants without a technical background?**

Yes, the programme is designed as a business-focused and non-technical offering. While advanced technologies such as AI and blockchain are discussed in depth, they are explained through managerial frameworks, business use cases, and simulations rather than through coding or technical implementation.

### Certification & Completion

**Q: Will I receive a certificate upon successful completion of the programme?**

Yes. Upon successful completion of the program and fulfillment of all academic requirements, participants will receive a digital copy of the certificate on their registered email Id.

**Q: When will I receive my certificate after completing the program?**

Certificates will be issued within 2–3 months from the date of the final examination. The exact timeline for issuance will be communicated during the program.

**Q: What are the completion or assessment criteria?**

To be eligible for certification, participants are required to maintain a minimum of 75% live attendance across the program and successfully complete at least 3 out of the 5 quizzes conducted during the course. These requirements are designed to ensure consistent participation and learning engagement throughout the programme.

### Mode of Delivery & Schedule

**Q: What is the mode of delivery for the programme?**

The programme is delivered entirely online through live sessions, allowing participants to attend from any location while benefiting from real-time faculty interaction.

**Q: What happens if I miss a live session?**

Participants are strongly encouraged to attend all live sessions to maximize learning outcomes. Guidelines related to session recordings and access duration will be communicated during the programme in accordance with institute policies.
