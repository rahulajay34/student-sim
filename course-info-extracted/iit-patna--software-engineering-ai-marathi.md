## Programming, DSA & AI-Powered Foundations

- Python Essentials for Modern Developers: Learn Python from scratch with a focus on writing clean, readable code that developers actually ship in real projects
- Thinking in Code Logic, Loops & Problem Solving: Master conditionals, loops and structured thinking to break down real-world problems into simple, executable steps
- Data Structures Fundamentals: Get hands-on with fundamental structures like arrays, strings, lists, dictionaries, and hashmaps
- Applied Algorithmic Thinking (Searching & Sorting): Focus on the intuition and complexity of core algorithms (e.g., binary search, insertion sort)
- Git & Collaboration Basics: Use Git and GitHub to version your code, collaborate with others and showcase your work like a real software engineer
- GenAI Coding Lab I: AI for DSA & Problem Solving (The Coding Alternative): Use AI assistants to explain complex DSA concepts, generate pseudocode for algorithms, and provide visual step-by-step solutions, focusing on understanding the intuition before implementation

## FAQs

### Program Overview

**Q: What is the program duration?**

The program spans 5 months, offering a well-paced and immersive learning experience designed to build both foundational and advanced skills through structured coursework, live sessions, and hands-on projects.

**Q: Will I receive a digital or physical copy of the programme certificate?**

Upon successful completion of the program and fulfillment of all academic requirements, participants will receive a digital copy of the certificate.

**Q: When will I receive my certificate after completing the program?**

Certificates will be issued within 2–3 months from the date of the final examination. The exact timeline for issuance will be communicated during the program.

**Q: What is the structure of the program (online, offline)?**

The mode of delivery of the program is online through the Zoom platform.

**Q: Who will be teaching the classes in this program?**

The sessions will be led by esteemed faculty members who bring strong academic and research expertise in and related domains. Alongside them, seasoned industry experts will conduct sessions to provide practical insights and real-world applications ensuring a comprehensive and hands-on learning experience.

### Eligibility & Admissions

**Q: What are the eligibility criteria for the program?**

Anyone with a minimum qualification of 12th grade (or equivalent) and a strong interest in building a career in Software Development with Applied AI is eligible to apply for the program.

**Q: Can international students apply for the program?**

Yes, international students can apply for the program, provided they meet the eligibility criteria set for the course.

**Q: What is the admission process?**

The admissions process begins with signing up and taking a Qualifier test. If you clear the test, you'll be invited for a one-on-one session where we'll walk you through the program overview and share the next steps to help you complete your enrollment smoothly.

**Q: What skills does the Qualifier test assess?**

The Qualifier test evaluates your logical reasoning, mathematical ability, quantitative aptitude, and communication skills which are basic requirements of the program.

**Q: Can I take the Qualifier test after my college exams are over?**

Seats are limited, so we strongly recommend taking the Qualifier test at the earliest to avoid missing the opportunity to kickstart your career in Software Development with Applied AI.

### Fees

**Q: What is the difference between Registration fee and Course fees?**

The Registration Fee secures your seat in the program. The Course Fee is the total program cost, which can be paid upfront or in EMIs with support from our NBFC partner. Your enrollment is confirmed only after the full Course Fee is paid.

**Q: How soon will I receive the receipt after paying the registration or course fee?**

The receipt will be emailed to your registered address within 24–48 business hours of payment.

**Q: Are there any specific financial institutions/partners involved in the EMI process?**

Yes, we've partnered with trusted NBFCs like Bajaj Finance, ShopSe, Varthana, Propelld, to support you with the EMI process.

**Q: What is the fee payment deadline?**

The fee payment deadline is available on your dashboard. Kindly ensure to complete the fee payment within the timelines to complete your enrolment in the program.

**Q: Is there any financial aid or scholarship available?**

Yes, scholarships are offered based on your performance in the Qualifier test. The program reserves the right to determine the eligibility and extent of the scholarship awarded to each candidate.

**Q: What is the rate of interest on the loan?**

The rate of interest depends upon multiple factors like tenure, amount, credit score, NBFC partner, etc. The details will be informed by the finance team for the program.

**Q: Is there any additional cost for admission kits?**

The kit cost of ₹200 is already included in the total course fee. There are no additional charges for admission kits.

**Q: Are there any additional charges for assessments during the course?**

The assessment cost of ₹300, which covers all assessments throughout the course, is already included in the total course fee. No extra charges will be applicable.

### Class Schedule

**Q: What are the class timings?**

The classes are scheduled on weekdays as well as weekends, designed with your time and priorities in mind. On weekdays, classes are scheduled from 8:00PM onwards and weekend timings are subject to faculty availability and schedule. The detailed schedule will be shared with you on the virtual inaugural day.

**Q: How many hours of engagement is required per week?**

You'll need to dedicate approximately 10 hours per week, which includes attending live sessions and tutorials, completing assignments and engaging in self-paced study to stay on track with the program.

**Q: How will the class schedule be communicated to students?**

The class schedule will be communicated primarily through the Learning Management System (LMS). It will also be shared on the official WhatsApp community.

**Q: What if I miss a live session, will I be able to access recording?**

We recommend you to attend Live sessions. In any foreseen circumstances if you miss attending live classes, recordings will be made accessible to you on the Learning Management System (LMS). Kindly ensure to watch the entire class recording to ensure you don't miss important concepts covered.

**Q: I am a working professional / college student and would like to plan my week ahead. Can I get access to the schedule well in advance?**

Yes, you'll receive the class schedule and updates every Saturday for the upcoming week, allowing you to plan your time effectively and stay organized.

**Q: Is Attendance mandatory for course completion?**

Yes, to be eligible for course completion, you must maintain a minimum of 65% attendance, which can be achieved through either live sessions or recorded lectures.

While we strongly recommend attending all live sessions to make the most of the program. In cases where you miss live classes, it's essential to watch the complete class recordings within 30 days to stay on track and ensure you grasp the concepts covered. Consistent participation is important for successful course completion and getting the best learning experience.

### Student Support

**Q: How can I raise any queries once I enroll in the program?**

Once your enrolment is complete, you will be onboarded on the Learning Management System (LMS). You can raise your queries through ticketing support and your program coordinator support with all non-conceptual queries during the tenure of the course.

**Q: How can I interact with my peers?**

You will have opportunities to engage throughout the program. You can connect with industry mentors and TAs during dedicated doubt-clearing sessions. Additionally, you can interact and collaborate with fellow learners through our learning platforms and private Discord community.

**Q: Is there a helpline session for student queries?**

Yes, a dedicated live helpline session is available to address your queries. Details of the session will be shared as part of your weekly schedule to help you stay informed and supported throughout the program.

**Q: What learning resources will be made available during the program?**

You will have access to a variety of learning resources, like notes, pre-reads, class recordings, assignments to support learning on Learning Management System (LMS).

**Q: How often will assessments be conducted?**

Assessments will be conducted periodically throughout the program, with evaluations at the end of each module and ongoing project work to track your progress.

### Placement Opportunities

**Q: Does the program offer placement assistance?**

If you score 7+ CGPA and above and meet the minimum attendance criteria of 65%, which can be achieved through either live sessions or recorded lectures you will be eligible for placement assistance from Masai School, the ed-tech and implementation partner of the program.

**Q: What is the average salary package for graduates of this program?**

Upon completion of the program, students will gain industry-relevant skills and hands-on experience with real-world tools. While average salary packages can vary based on factors such as your educational background, prior experience, and the role or company you pursue, certification from a prestigious IIT-linked institution like iHub – IIT Patna significantly enhances your profile and boosts your chances of securing high-potential job opportunities in the AI and tech industry.

**Q: What kind of support will I receive to prepare for job interviews?**

If you meet the required performance and engagement criteria during the program, you'll receive dedicated support to get industry-ready. This includes resume building, LinkedIn profile enhancement, and mock AI-based interviews equipping you with the tools and confidence to make the most of available career opportunities.
