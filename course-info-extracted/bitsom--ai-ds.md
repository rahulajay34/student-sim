Module 1: Mathematics for Data Science

- • Linear Algebra: Vectors, matrices, system of equations, eigenvectors, orthogonality
- • Calculus: Functions, derivatives, chain rule, gradient, Jacobian, Hessian
- • Probability & Statistics: Conditional probability, Bayes’ theorem, random variables, expectation & variance, CLT, LLN
- • Optimization: Convex sets & functions, constrained/unconstrained optimization
- • Tools : Python (NumPy, SciPy)

Module 2: Machine Learning Fundamentals

- • Supervised Learning: Regression (Linear, Ridge, Lasso), classification (Logistic Regression, SVM, Decision Trees, k-NN)
- • Unsupervised Learning: Clustering (K-Means, GMM), Dimensionality Reduction (PCA)
- • Model Evaluation: Accuracy, Precision, Recall, F1, ROC, AUC, Cross-validation, Bias-Variance Trade-off
- • Neural Networks Basics: Perceptrons, loss functions, forward/backpropagation
- • Training Methods: SGD, Adam, Batch Norm, Dropout, Regularization
- • Tools: Python, Scikit-learn, Google Colab

Module 3: Advanced Deep Learning & AI Applications

- • Deep Learning Architectures: CNNs (ResNet, Inception), RNNs (LSTM, GRU), Transformers (BERT, GPT)
- • Generative Models: VAEs, GANs (CycleGANs, Conditional GANs)
- • Reinforcement Learning: Agents, rewards, Q-Learning, Policy Gradients, Deep RL (DQN, Actor-Critic)
- • Applications: NLP (tokenization, word embeddings, sentiment analysis, NER), Computer Vision (Object Detection, Segmentation)
- • Emerging Trends: Self-supervised learning, multi-task learning, ethics in AI
- • Tools: TensorFlow, PyTorch, Hugging Face Transformers

Module 4: Capstone Project + Industry Expert Sessions

- • Develop an end-to-end AI solution using real-world data.
- • Domains: NLP Chatbots, Computer Vision Apps, Recommendation Engines, Generative Models.
- • Monthly live sessions with industry mentors for project feedback and career guidance.
- • Final presentation & deployment with faculty evaluation.

## FAQs

### Programme Overview

**Q: What is the program duration?**

The program spans <strong>6 months</strong>, offering a structured and immersive learning journey designed to equip you with advanced expertise in Artificial Intelligence and Data Science.

**Q: What topics and concepts are covered in the program?**

The BITSoM <strong>Artificial Intelligence & Data Science Mastery Program</strong>, in collaboration with Masai School, features a comprehensive curriculum covering both foundational and advanced concepts. Key areas include Python programming, data analysis, machine learning, deep learning, natural language processing, computer vision, and AI ethics. The program also emphasizes practical applications of generative and agentic AI to solve real-world business challenges.

**Q: What are the projects I will work on?**

You will work on industry-relevant projects such as predictive analytics, recommendation engines, image and text classification, business forecasting models, and end-to-end AI-powered solutions(The Projects are subject to change). These projects are designed to help you apply your learning in practical scenarios and build a strong portfolio.

**Q: Will I receive a digital or physical copy of the programme certificate?**

Upon successful completion of the program and fulfillment of all requirements, participants will receive a digital copy of the certificate. The digital certificate will be dispatched to the registered address shared during enrollment.

**Q: When will I receive my certificate after completing the program?**

Certificates will be issued within 2–3 months from the date of the final examination. The exact timeline for issuance will be communicated during the program.

**Q: What is the mode of delivery for the program?**

The program is delivered entirely <strong>online via Zoom</strong> , providing you with flexibility to learn from anywhere while engaging in live, interactive sessions.

**Q: Who will be teaching the classes in this program?**

The sessions will be led by <strong>BITSoM faculty members</strong> with deep expertise in AI, data science, and computational techniques. In addition, industry practitioners and domain experts will conduct applied sessions, ensuring a well-rounded, practice-oriented learning experience. 

**Q: What tools and software will I learn during the course?**

You will gain hands-on experience with widely used tools and libraries such as <strong>Python, NumPy, Pandas, Scikit-learn, TensorFlow, PyTorch, SQL, Tableau, and Power BI</strong>. These are essential for performing advanced analytics, building models, and deploying AI-driven solutions.

**Q: Will I work on real-world case studies?**

Yes, the program integrates <strong>real-world case studies</strong> across domains such as finance, healthcare, retail, technology, etc. These case studies allow you to apply your knowledge to industry-relevant challenges and develop problem-solving skills.

**Q: What is campus immersion?**

The 2-day campus immersion is an optional in-person immersive experience designed to enhance learning through instructor interaction, campus exposure and networking opportunities. The immersion may include AI-focused sessions, lab visits, faculty interactions, and curated academic engagements.

**Q: Is campus immersion mandatory, and when does it take place?**

It is typically scheduled towards the end of the program or after completion. While not mandatory, participation is highly recommended. The schedule will be shared in advance to enable travel planning.

**Q: What is the fee for the campus immersion?**

The campus immersion fee is ₹6,000 for Non-Residential participation and ₹10,000 for Residential participation per day. This fee covers one day of stay for residential participants and includes lunch and refreshments provided during the scheduled immersion hours.<br><br>The immersion fee is not included in the programme fee and must be paid separately by participants. Travel expenses are not included and must be arranged and borne individually by the participants.

Anyone with a minimum qualification of 12th grade (or equivalent) and a strong interest in building a career in Artificial Intelligence & Data Science is eligible to apply for the program.

Yes, international students can apply for the program, provided they meet the eligibility criteria set for the course.

The admissions process begins with signing up and taking a Qualifier test. If you clear the test, you'll be invited for a one-on-one session where we’ll walk you through the program overview and share the next steps to help you complete your enrollment smoothly.

The Qualifier test evaluates your Verbal Ability, Quantitative Aptitude, and Statistics, which are basic requirements of the program.

**Q: Can I take the Qualifier test after my college exams are over?**

Seats are limited, so we strongly recommend taking the Qualifier test at the earliest to avoid missing the opportunity to kickstart your career in digital marketing.

### Class Schedule

**Q: What are the class timings?**

The classes are scheduled on weekdays as well as weekends, designed with your time and priorities in mind. On weekdays, classes are scheduled from 8:00PM onwards and weekend timings are subject to professor’s availability and schedule. The detailed schedule will be shared with you on Day 1 of the program.

**Q: How many hours of engagement is required per week?**

You’ll need to dedicate approximately 8–10 hours per week, which includes attending live sessions(3-4 hours), completing tutorials, and engaging in self-paced study to stay on track with the program.

**Q: How will the class schedule be communicated to students?**

The class schedule will be communicated primarily through the Learning Management System (LMS). It will also be shared on the official WhatsApp community.

**Q: What if I miss a live session , will I be able to access recording?**

We recommend you to attend Live sessions. In any foreseen circumstances if you miss attending live classes, recordings will be made accessible to you on the Learning Management System (LMS). Kindly ensure to watch the entire class recording to ensure you don't miss important concepts covered.

**Q: I am a working professional / college student and would like to plan my week ahead. Can I get access to the schedule well in advance?**

Yes, you will receive the class schedule and updates well in advance, allowing you to plan your time effectively and stay organized.

**Q: Is there any attendance criteria in this program for course completion?**

Yes, to be eligible for course completion, you must maintain a minimum of 65% attendance, which can be achieved through either live sessions or recorded lectures.

**Q: How is attendance calculated?**

Attendance is calculated automatically through the Learning Management System (LMS). You will be marked present if you complete either one of the following in full:<br><br><strong>Live Attendance</strong> – Counted when you join the live session in full using your registered email ID and attend the class.<br><br><strong>Recording Attendance</strong> – Counted when you watch the session recording in full within 30 days of the class.

**Q: What happens if the student doesn’t meet the required attendance criteria?**

If a student does not meet the required attendance criteria, they will not be eligible to receive the course completion certificate.

**Q: What if I am unable to attend live sessions or watch recordings within 30 days due to some exigencies?**

In such cases, you can raise a support ticket with your respective Program Coordinator, along with the necessary supporting documents. Exceptions may be granted after reviewing your request.
