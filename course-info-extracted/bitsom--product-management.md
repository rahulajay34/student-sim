#### Certification

From BITSoM and PwC Academy

Module 01: Product Thinking & Opportunity Discovery

- • Product Management fundamentals and strategic thinking
- • Market & environment scanning to spot opportunities
- • Structured ideation and proposition building
- • Feasibility analysis and platform strategy basics

Module 02: Design & MVP

- • Design thinking with personas and user journeys
- • Feature prioritisation and MVP planning
- • Prototyping with strong UX/UI foundations
- • Tech selection, development flow, and release basics

Module 03: Building with Agentic AI

- • Foundations of agentic AI for product thinking
- • Prompt engineering (core + advanced patterns, including RAG)
- • Agent frameworks, tool invocation, and multimodal workflows
- • LLM-driven prototyping, no/low-code agents, LLMOps, and Responsible AI

Module 04: Project Management and Agile Execution

- • Project management foundations and delivery approaches
- • Agile principles with Scrum framework, roles, and ceremonies
- • Roadmaps, backlog management, prioritisation, and Jira usage
- • Stakeholder alignment, communication, and ethics in product work

Module 05: Go-To-Market

- • Marketing strategy frameworks and branding fundamentals
- • Promotion mix (7P) and digital marketing essentials
- • Consumer behaviour and growth acceleration levers
- • Sales, distribution strategy, and sustainable product thinking

Module 06: Data, Metrics & Decision Making

- • Pricing strategy and customer service fundamentals
- • Market research and data-driven decision-making basics
- • Core analytics including regression (applied understanding)
- • Product metrics, media metrics, CLV, and A/B testing

Capstone Project

- • Design and build an AI-enhanced product solving a real user problem
- • Apply product thinking: discovery, MVP, build and launch approach
- • Integrate Generative AI and agentic systems into the product solution

## FAQs

### Program Overview

**Q: What is the Product Management and Agentic AI programme by BITSoM about?**

It is a six‑month experiential course that equips aspiring product managers and working professionals with practical skills in product management plus Agentic AI. You learn user research, UI/UX, product‑lifecycle management and how to design AI‑powered products through projects and case studies.

**Q: What is the programme duration?**

The programme spans six months and is structured to provide an immersive, job‑ready learning journey.

**Q: What topics and concepts are covered in the programme?**

The curriculum blends product‑management foundations (user research, prioritisation frameworks, stakeholder collaboration, data‑driven decision‑making) with Agentic‑AI design. You will work with intelligent agents for automation and personalisation, explore product discovery, design, deployment and go‑to‑market strategy across domains such as e‑commerce and tech.

**Q: What are the  projects I will work on?**

You will complete a capstone project where you design and develop an AI‑driven product or feature (e.g., an intelligent agent or personalised recommender). Supporting projects include persona creation, prototype design and product‑journey mapping.

**Q: Will I receive a certificate upon completion?**

Yes, upon successful completion, you will receive a certificate from BITS School of Management awarded based on your performance and fulfillment of the program's completion criteria.

**Q: What is the mode of delivery for the programme?**

All sessions are delivered live online via Zoom.

**Q: Who will be teaching the classes in this programme?**

The sessions will be led by esteemed faculty members of BITSoM, along with industry experts, who bring extensive expertise in product management, agentic AI, and generative AI, ensuring a rich blend of academic rigor and industry-relevant insights throughout the program.

**Q: What tools and software will I learn during the course?**

You get hands‑on with Figma, Jira, Trello and Agentic‑AI frameworks such as Langflow or CrewAI. You will also use analytics platforms like Mixpanel or Amplitude.

**Q: Will I work on real-world case studies?**

Yes, especially in the capstone module, where you will solve a real-world problem using Agentic AI and generative AI to industry-relevant challenges.

**Q: What is campus immersion? Is it mandatory?**

Campus immersion is an enriching on-campus experience that allows you to interact with professors and peers, and strengthen your professional network. While it is not mandatory, it is highly recommended. The schedule will be shared in advance so you can plan your travel accordingly. Please note that any costs for accommodation, food, and travel will be borne by the students and are not included in the course fees.

### Eligibility & Admissions

**Q: What are the eligibility criteria for the program?**

Anyone with a minimum qualification of 12th grade (or equivalent) and a strong interest in building a career in Product Management or AI-driven tech roles is eligible to apply. A basic understanding of technology or product concepts is recommended but not mandatory.

### Class Schedule

**Q: What are the class timings?**

The classes are primarily scheduled on weekends, and a few classes may be held on weekdays after 8:00 PM. The schedule is subject to change based on the professors’ availability.

**Q: How many hours of engagement is required per week?**

You'll need to dedicate approximately 8 hours per week, which includes attending live sessions, completing tutorials, and engaging in self-paced study to stay on track with the program.

**Q: How will the class schedule be communicated to students?**

The class schedule will be communicated primarily through the Learning Management System (LMS). It will also be shared on the official WhatsApp community.

**Q: What if I miss a live session, will I be able to access the recording?**

We recommend attending live sessions. In any unforeseen circumstances, if you miss a live class, recordings will be made accessible on the Learning Management System (LMS). Kindly ensure to watch the entire class recording to avoid missing important concepts covered.

**Q: I am a working professional / college student and would like to plan my week ahead. Can I get access to the schedule well in advance?**

Yes, you'll receive the class schedule and updates every Saturday for the upcoming week, allowing you to plan your time effectively and stay organized.

**Q: What happens if the student doesn’t meet the required attendance criteria?**

If a student does not meet the required attendance criteria, they will not be eligible to receive the course completion certificate.

**Q: Is Attendance mandatory for course completion?**

While we strongly recommend attending all live sessions to make the most of the program, in cases where you miss live classes, it's essential to watch the complete class recordings to stay on track and ensure you grasp the concepts covered. Consistent participation is important for successful course completion and getting the best learning experience.

**Q: How is attendance calculated?**

Attendance is calculated automatically through the Learning Management System (LMS). You will be marked present if you complete either one of the following in full:<br><br><strong>Live Attendance</strong> – Counted when you join the live session in full using your registered email ID and attend the class.<br><br><strong>Recording Attendance</strong> – Counted when you watch the session recording in full within 30 days of the class.

**Q: What happens if the student doesn't meet the required attendance criteria?**

If a student does not meet the required attendance criteria, they will not be eligible to receive the course completion certificate.

**Q: What if I am unable to attend live sessions or watch recordings within 30 days due to some exigencies?**

In such cases, you can raise a support ticket with your respective Program Coordinator, along with the necessary supporting documents. Exceptions may be granted after reviewing your request.

### Student Support

**Q: How can I raise any queries once I enroll in the program?**

Once your enrollment is complete, you will be onboarded on the Learning Management System (LMS). You can raise your queries through ticketing support and program coordinator support for all non-conceptual queries during the tenure of the course.

**Q: How can I interact with my peers?**

You will have group projects during the program wherein you will get the opportunity to connect with your peers, exchange ideas, collaborate, and learn from each other.

**Q: Is there a helpline session for student queries?**

Yes, a dedicated live helpline session is available to address your queries. Details of the session will be shared as part of your weekly schedule to help you stay informed and supported throughout the program.

**Q: What learning resources will be made available during the program?**

You will have access to a variety of learning resources, such as notes, pre-reads, class recordings, and assignments to support learning on the Learning Management System (LMS).

**Q: How often will assessments be conducted?**

Assessments will be conducted periodically throughout the program, with evaluations at the end of each module and ongoing Assignments will be released to track your progress.

**Q: What is the certification criteria?**

To be eligible for the certificate, you are required to maintain a minimum of 65% attendance, including the time spent watching recorded sessions. You must also achieve a minimum passing percentage of 50%. The final exam will account for 50% of your grade, assignments and quizzes will make up 20%, and the mid-semester exam will contribute 30%. Consistent participation and meeting these requirements are essential to ensure eligibility for certification.
