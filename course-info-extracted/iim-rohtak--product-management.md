# What Will You Learn?

Elevate your career with the AI-Powered Product Management & Growth Marketing Program. This intensive course equips you to build, launch, market, and scale digital products. Master agentic AI, using ChatGPT, OpenAI Assistants API, and Midjourney for automation and personalization. Learn product thinking, UX, digital growth channels, and apply AI-driven strategies to your campaigns. The program culminates in a Capstone Project to launch a digital product, ensuring you gain a competitive edge in data-driven leadership.

####

From IIM Rohtak

Module 1: Marketing Fundamentals & Branding

- • ICPs and market segmentation, Positioning, Brand storytelling. Tools: HubSpot, Canva.

Module 2: Product Thinking

- • Product concept, Concept maps, concept combination table, Product lifecycle.

Module 3: UX, Prototyping & Product Analytics

- • Wireframes, user flows, prototyping, Usability testing, Retention analysis. Tools: Figma, Hotjar.

Module 4: Business Application of AI in Product & Marketing

- • AI-driven personalization, Journey orchestration using LLMs, Predictive analytics. What is Agentic AI, Agentic vs traditional AI, properties of autonomous agents, Assistants API, and human-in-the-loop AI ethics. Tools: ChatGPT, OpenAI Assistants API, Midjourney, Looker Studio.

Module 5: Digital Strategy for Products

- • Problem-solution fit, competitor analysis, business model canvas, strategic canvas.

Module 6: Digital Growth Channels

- • Performance marketing, Paid ads (Meta, Google), SEO/SEM, email, influencer marketing, Campaign optimization and performance marketing. Tools: Meta Ads, Google Ads.

Module 7: Agile, Roadmaps & Stakeholder Management

- • Roadmapping, Sprint planning, Stakeholder management. Tools: Jira, Notion.

Module 8: Integrative Project

- • Team-based project to build a roadmap on product MVP, launch, and growth.

## FAQs

### Program Overview

**Q: What is the duration of the program?**

The program runs for 5 months, offering an intensive learning experience focused on AI-powered Product Management, Product Marketing, Growth strategy, and hands-on implementation.

**Q: Will I receive a digital or physical copy of the programme certificate?**

Upon successful completion of the program and fulfillment of all academic requirements, participants will receive a physical copy of the certificate. The physical certificate will be dispatched to the registered address shared during enrollment.

**Q: When will I receive my certificate after completing the program?**

Certificates will be issued within 2–3 months from the date of the final examination. The exact timeline for issuance will be communicated during the program.

**Q: What is the mode of delivery for the program?**

The program is delivered entirely online, with live sessions conducted via Zoom, as stated in the Learning Mode section.

**Q: Who will be teaching the classes in this program?**

The sessions will be conducted by:
- IIM Rohtak faculty, known for academic depth in management and strategic domains.
- Industry experts, who will complement the curriculum with practical real-world insights.

**Q: What is campus immersion?**

The 2-3 day campus immersion is an optional in-person immersive experience designed to enhance learning through instructor interaction, campus exposure and networking opportunities.

**Q: Is campus immersion mandatory, and when does it take place?**

It is typically scheduled towards the end of the program or after completion. While not mandatory, participation is highly recommended. The schedule will be shared in advance to enable travel planning.

**Q: What is the fee for the campus immersion?**

The immersion fee depends on the accommodation option selected. The fee is ₹3,650 per day for Double Occupancy and ₹4,850 per day for Single Occupancy. This amount is not included in the course fees and must be borne additionally by students.<br><br>Travel expenses must be borne by students and are not included in the campus immersion fees.

### Fees & Payment

**Q: What is the difference between the Registration fee and the Course fees?**

The Registration Fee secures your seat in the program, while the Course Fee is the total program fee, which can be paid in full upfront or through EMIs with the help of a financing partner.

**Q: When do I receive the receipt after paying the fee?**

The receipt will be emailed to you within 24-48 business hours after the payment is processed.

**Q: Are there any specific financial institutions/partners involved in the EMI process?**

Yes, we have partnered with trusted NBFCs like Bajaj Finance, Propelld, and Varthana to support you with EMI options for fee payment.

**Q: What is the fee payment deadline?**

The fee payment deadline is available on your dashboard. Please ensure to complete the payment within the given timeline to confirm your enrollment in the program.

**Q: What is the rate of interest on the loan?**

The interest rate for the loan depends on factors such as loan tenure, loan amount, and credit score. The loan processing team will provide you with detailed information based on the financing partner.

**Q: Is there any additional cost for admission kits?**

The kit cost of ₹600 is already included in the total course fee. There are no additional charges for admission kits.

**Q: Are there any additional charges for assessments during the course?**

The assessment cost of ₹1400, which covers all assessments throughout the course, is already included in the total course fee. No extra charges will be applicable.

### Class Schedule & Learning Format

**Q: What are the class timings?**

The classes are scheduled on weekends to accommodate working professionals and students. The exact timings will be shared with you on the first day of the program.

**Q: How many hours of engagement are required per week?**

You will need to dedicate approximately 8-10 hours per week for live sessions, tutorials, and self-paced study.

**Q: How will the class schedule be communicated to students?**

The class schedule will be shared via the Learning Management System (LMS) and will also be posted on the official WhatsApp community.

**Q: What if I miss a live session? Will I be able to access the recording?**

Yes, the recordings of all live sessions will be available on the LMS. It is highly recommended to attend the live sessions, but you can catch up on missed sessions by watching the recordings.

**Q: Is attendance mandatory for course completion?**

While attending live sessions is strongly recommended, you can still complete the program by watching the recorded sessions. Consistent participation is important for successful course completion and certification.

### Placement Opportunities

**Q: Does the program offer placement opportunities?**

Yes. Learners who score 7+ CGPA or above and maintain a minimum of 70% attendance throughout the program will be eligible for placement oppurtunities provided by Masai School, the ed-tech and implementation partner for this program.

**Q: What is the average salary package for graduates of this program?**

Graduates receive a certificate from IIM Rohtak, which adds strong credibility to their profile and can positively impact career opportunities. Salary outcomes vary based on multiple factors such as prior work experience, educational background, role pursued, industry, company, and individual performance. Hence, there is no fixed or guaranteed average salary associated with the program.

**Q: What kind of support will I receive to prepare for job interviews?**

Learners who meet the required performance and engagement criteria will receive structured career support aimed at improving industry readiness. This includes resume building and optimization, and mock interviews using AI-based tools. This support is designed to help learners present their skills effectively and confidently during hiring processes.

### Student Support & Learning Experience

**Q: How can I raise any queries once I enroll in the program?**

You can raise queries via the LMS ticketing support system or contact your Program Coordinator for non-conceptual questions during the course.

**Q: How can I interact with my peers?**

You will have opportunities to engage throughout the program. You can connect with industry mentors and TAs during dedicated doubt-clearing sessions. Additionally, you can interact and collaborate with fellow learners through our learning platforms and private Discord community.

**Q: Is there a helpline session for student queries?**

Yes, a dedicated helpline session is available to address your queries. The details will be shared as part of your weekly schedule.

**Q: What learning resources will be available during the program?**

You will have access to lecture notes, pre-reads, class recordings, and assignments through the LMS, supporting your learning experience.

**Q: How often will assessments be conducted?**

Assessments will be conducted periodically, with evaluations at the end of each module and ongoing project work to track your progress.
