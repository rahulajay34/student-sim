# What will you learn?

This program offers a practical, step-by-step roadmap to launching and scaling a startup. You'll master everything from idea validation and product development to digital marketing, funding, and operations, guided by IIM Mumbai’s expert faculty and real-world entrepreneurs.

### Course Details

#### Duration

4 Months

#### Course Mode

#### Certification

From IIM Mumbai

Module 1: Introduction to Entrepreneurship

- • This module introduces the fundamentals of entrepreneurship, covering the mindset required to become a successful entrepreneur. You will learn how to create a business model, identify opportunities, and test your ideas to validate your business concepts

Module 2: How to Innovate and Grasp Customer Needs

- • In this module, you will explore how to deeply understand customer needs and how innovation can solve their problems. You will learn how to conduct effective market research and design solutions based on customer insights

Module 3: Project Management for Startups

- • This module covers the essential skills of project management in a startup environment. You'll learn how to manage resources, time, and risks, ensuring your startup projects stay on track and meet their goals.

Module 4: Product Development

- • Focus on the product development lifecycle, from ideation to market-ready products. This module will help you understand how to create prototypes, gather user feedback, and iterate on your product to meet customer demands.

Module 5: Technology Decision Making for Startups

- • Learn to make strategic decisions about the technology selection. This module will teach you how to choose scalable and cost-effective technology solutions that align with your startup's goals.

Module 6: Digital Marketing

- • Explore the world of digital marketing and how to use it effectively for your startup. Learn the appropriate techniques, social media marketing, and advertising to build your brand and attract customers.

Module 7: Minimum Viable Product: The Lean Approach to Product Development

- • The Lean Approach to Product Development This module introduces the Lean Startup methodology, which focuses on building a Minimum Viable Product (MVP). Learn how to validate your ideas quickly, minimize waste, and scale your product effectively.

Module 8: Types of Startup Funding

- • Understand the different funding options available to startups, from venture capital to crowdfunding. Learn how to choose the right funding sources for your startup and how to pitch to investors effectively.

Module 9: Financial Planning for Startups

- • Learn how to create a solid financial plan for your startup. This module will teach you about budgeting, forecasting, managing cash flow, and fundraising strategies to ensure your startup's financial health

Module 10: Protecting Your Idea Through IP

- • Intellectual Property (IP) protection is crucial for startups. This module explains how to secure your innovations through patents, trademarks, and copyrights, etc. ensuring that your ideas are legally protected.

Module 11: Startup Leadership

- • Effective leadership is key to the success of any startup. In this module, you'll learn leadership strategies that help in building, managing, and motivating a team to drive growth and success.

Module 12: Startup Operations

- • This module focuses on the operational aspects of running a startup. Learn how to set up effective processes, scale operations, and manage the day-to-day functions of your startup to ensure its long-term success

## FAQs

### Program Overview

**Q: What is the program duration?**

The program spans 4 Months, offering a well-paced and immersive learning experience designed to build both foundational and advanced skills through structured coursework, live sessions, and case studies.

**Q: What topics and concepts are covered in the program?**

The IIM Mumbai Certificate Program in Entrepreneurship and Start Up Mastery, in collaboration with NSDC and Masai, covers entrepreneurial mindset, business model development, innovation frameworks, market analysis, funding strategies, scaling ventures, and legal aspects of startups. You'll explore lean startup methodologies, customer validation, and emerging trends like AI-driven innovation, equipping you with practical skills to launch and grow successful businesses.

**Q: What are the projects I will work on?**

You will engage in real-world case studies discussed in class, analyzing scenarios such as startup scaling strategies, funding pitches, and innovation challenges in industries like technology and retail. A capstone project involves developing and pitching a startup idea, applying concepts like business modeling and market validation.

**Q: Will I receive a certificate upon completion?**

Yes, upon successful completion of the program, you will receive a certificate from IIM Mumbai. This certification will be awarded based on your overall performance and fulfillment of the program's completion criteria.

**Q: What will be the criteria to get the certificate?**

A Completion Certificate will be awarded to participants who maintain at least 75% attendance and secure a minimum of 60% in the evaluation test.

**Q: Can participants receive a certificate without completing the full program?**

A Participation Certificate will be given to those with at least 50% attendance.

**Q: What is the structure of the program (online, offline)?**

The mode of delivery of the program is online through the Zoom platform.

**Q: Who will be teaching the classes in this program?**

The sessions will be led by esteemed faculty members of IIM Mumbai, renowned for their expertise in entrepreneurship, innovation, and business strategy, alongside industry experts who bring practical insights from successful startups and corporate innovation, ensuring a comprehensive learning experience.

**Q: What tools and software will I learn during the course?**

You will gain hands-on experience with essential tools used in the startup world. These include Canva or PowerPoint for creating impactful pitch decks, Google Workspace and Excel for collaboration, financial planning, and modeling, and platforms like Trello or Notion for project management.

**Q: Will I work on real-world case studies?**

Yes, the program includes real-world case studies discussed in class, such as startup scaling strategies, funding pitches, and innovation challenges in industries like technology and retail, allowing you to apply entrepreneurship and innovation concepts to address industry-relevant problems.

**Q: Can I apply for the course if I do not have a background in business?**

Yes, the course is designed to help beginners understand the fundamentals of entrepreneurship, business strategy, and startup management. Prior business knowledge is not a requirement.

**Q: What is the Ideathon at IIM Mumbai? Am I required to participate?**

The Ideathon at IIM Mumbai is a dynamic, competitive event where students collaborate, brainstorm, and present innovative solutions to real-world challenges. Participation is not mandatory and is limited to students who meet predefined academic and program-related eligibility criteria. Please note that any costs for accommodation, food, and travel will be borne by the students and are not included in the course fees.

### Eligibility & Admissions

**Q: What are the eligibility criteria for the program?**

Anyone currently pursuing graduation in any discipline (or equivalent) or those who have already completed graduation, with a strong interest in entrepreneurship and startup mastery, are eligible to apply for the program. Applicants must have achieved a minimum of 55% marks in their most recent qualifying examination to be considered for admission.

**Q: Can international students apply for the program?**

Yes, international students can apply for the program, provided they meet the eligibility criteria set for the course.

**Q: What is the admission process?**

The admissions process begins with signing up and taking a Qualifier test. If you clear the test, you'll be invited for a one-on-one session where we'll walk you through the program overview and share the next steps to help you complete your enrollment smoothly.

**Q: What skills does the Qualifier test assess?**

The Qualifier test assesses your skills in vocabulary within a business context, motivation and entrepreneurial mindset, logical thinking and business math, mini case studies, and general awareness, all of which are essential for the program.

**Q: Can I take the Qualifier test after my college exams are over?**

Seats are limited, so we strongly recommend taking the Qualifier test at the earliest to avoid missing the opportunity to kickstart your career in entrepreneurship and startup mastery.
