# What will you learn?

In the Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning with Autonomous Vehicle Technology program from TiHAN - IIT Hyderabad, you will build a strong foundation in data science, explore core machine learning techniques, and understand ethical AI practices. Specialize in autonomous systems, focusing on perception, navigation, and real-time decision-making. Apply your learning through hands-on projects, developing intelligent solutions for real-world mobility challenges.

### Course Details

6 Months

#### Certification

from TiHAN IIT Hyderabad

Module 1: Data Science Fundamentals and Python for AIML

- • Build confidence in core Python fundamentals such as variables, data types,conditionals, loops, functions, and file handling
- • Understand structured data, descriptive statistics, probability, and foundational data science concepts
- • Work with NumPy, Pandas, and visualization tools for data handling and interpretation
- • Develop practical data literacy through handling and exploring simple datasets
- • Build the programming and data foundations required for later AIML modules

Module 2: Core Machine Learning and Predictive Modeling

- • Understand the basics of supervised and unsupervised learning
- • Apply foundational predictive models such as Linear Regression, Logistic Regression, Decision Trees, and Random Forests
- • Work with preprocessing, feature preparation, and model-ready datasets
- • Use evaluation metrics and model comparison methods to assess performance
- • Build confidence in using machine learning methods for practical prediction tasks

Module 3: Deep Learning, Computer Vision, and Perception

- • Understand the intuition behind neural networks and how they differ from classical machine learning models
- • Build familiarity with deep learning concepts and their role in perception-oriented problems
- • Recognize the role of image data and computer vision in intelligent systems
- • Work with perception-related workflows and visual interpretation tasks
- • Connect deep learning and perception concepts to autonomous systems applications

Module 4: Machine Learning Best Practices, Model Improvement, and Deployment

- • Understand how to structure machine learning projects from problem definition to model selection and improvement
- • Recognize the impact of data quality, class imbalance, leakage, and feature preparation on model performance
- • Interpret overfitting, underfitting, generalization, and model error through practical examples
- • Build familiarity with model tuning, experiment tracking, and comparison of alternative approaches
- • Understand deployment basics, inference workflows, model monitoring, and reliability in real-world AIML systems

Module 5: Emerging AI Applications: LLMs, GenAI, and AI Agents

- • Understand the key concepts behind LLMs, Generative AI, and AI agents
- • Recognize how modern AI systems differ from classical machine learning workflows
- • Identify practical use cases of GenAI and agent-based systems across industries
- • Understand how language-based AI, perception, and decision-making connect in intelligent systems
- • Relate emerging AI applications to autonomous systems and intelligent mobility use cases

Capstone Project

- • Capstone brief: Learners will complete an applied AIML project that demonstrates their ability to work with data, apply machine learning or perception-oriented logic, and interpret an intelligent systems or autonomous vehicles use case in a structured way
- • Define a practical problem and identify relevant data or system inputs
- • Apply appropriate AIML methods using the tools and concepts learned in the program
- • Communicate outputs, limitations, and possible improvements clearly
- • Integrate an autonomous systems or intelligent mobility lens into the project framing where appropriate

## FAQs

### Program Overview

**Q: What is the program duration?**

The program spans approximately 6 months, offering a structured and immersive learning journey across five modules, a campus immersion, and a capstone project designed to build strong foundations in Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning, and Autonomous Vehicle technologies. Autonomous vehicles–related topics include sensors, perception, ROS (Robot Operating System), and autonomous navigation technologies. Hands-on exercises and case-based learning reinforce practical understanding.

**Q: What are the projects I will work on?**

The capstone project involves solving an applied AIML problem. Learners will define a problem, work with relevant data or inputs, apply machine learning, and present outcomes along with limitations and improvements. Other projects include Python programming exercises, data analysis tasks, exploratory data analysis, and building regression and classification models aligned with real-world use cases.

**Q: Will I receive a digital or physical copy of the programme certificate?**

Upon successful completion of the program and fulfillment of all requirements, participants will receive a digital copy of the certificate. The digital certificate will be shared to the registered email ID shared during enrollment.

**Q: When will I receive my certificate after completing the program?**

Certificates will be issued on eligibility within 2–3 months from the date of the final evaluation. The exact timeline for issuance will be communicated during the program.

**Q: What is the mode of delivery for the program?**

The program is delivered online through live sessions, supported by hands-on projects, practical exercises, and guided tutorials.

**Q: Who will be teaching the classes in this program?**

The sessions will be led by experienced IIT faculty(any IIT Institute) and industry mentors with expertise in Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning, and autonomous systems, ensuring a balance of academic depth and practical application.

**Q: What tools and software will I learn during the course?**

You will gain hands-on experience with tools such as Python, Google Colab, NumPy, Pandas, and Matplotlib. You will also be introduced to machine learning workflows and tools, along with exposure to ROS for understanding autonomous systems and navigation.

**Q: Will I work on real-world case studies?**

Yes, the program includes real-world case studies and applied exercises that help you understand how AI and ML concepts are used in practical scenarios, including autonomous systems and intelligent mobility.

**Q: What is campus immersion?**

The 2-day campus immersion is an optional, in-person experience featuring exposure to autonomous ground-vehicle navigation, ROS workshops, and demonstrations of autonomous systems, along with interaction and networking opportunities. <br> <strong> Eligibility:</strong> Learners must have 50%+ marks in evaluations as of the immersion date.

**Q: Is campus immersion mandatory, and when does it take place?**

It is typically scheduled after the completion of all the modules. While not mandatory, participation is highly recommended. The schedule will be shared in advance to enable travel planning.

**Q: What is the fee for the campus immersion?**

The campus immersion fee is ₹5,000 per participant per day. This fee covers the arrangements at the immersion venue during the scheduled immersion hours.<br>The immersion fee is not included in the programme fee and must be paid separately by participants. Accommodation and travel expenses must be arranged and borne by students and are not included in the campus immersion fees.

**Q: Are food and accommodation included in the immersion fee?**

No. Food and accommodation are not included. Students are responsible for arranging and bearing these costs.

### Eligibility & Admissions

**Q: What are the eligibility criteria for the program?**

Anyone with a minimum qualification of 12th grade (or equivalent) and a strong interest in building a career in Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning, or autonomous systems is eligible to apply. A basic understanding of programming or mathematics is recommended but not mandatory.

**Q: Can international students apply for the program?**

Yes, international students can apply, provided they meet the eligibility criteria for the course. Please note that classes will be conducted as per Indian Standard Time (IST), typically on Saturdays and Sundays, mostly in the afternoon/evening based on professor availability.

The admission process begins with signing up and taking a Qualifier test/SOP Application submission. If you clear the test, you'll be invited for a one-on-one session where the program overview and next steps for enrollment will be shared to complete the process smoothly.

**Q: What skills does the Qualifier test assess?**

The Qualifier test evaluates your Logical reasoning, Quantitative Aptitude, Basic Maths, which are foundational requirements for the program.

**Q: Can I take the Qualifier test after my college exams are over?**

Seats are limited, so we strongly recommend taking the Qualifier test at the earliest to avoid missing the opportunity to begin your journey in Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning.
