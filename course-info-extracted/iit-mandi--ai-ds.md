# What will you learn?

The AI and Data Science program by CCE, IIT Mandi, a 15-credit course, offers a comprehensive curriculum covering foundational programming, mathematics, and statistics, progressing to advanced topics like machine learning, neural networks, and big data analytics. Designed for beginners with basic knowledge, it is taught by renowned IIT professors, ensuring high-quality learning and career-ready skills. MTH 101 Mathematics for Data Science

- • Learn core linear algebra concepts like vectors, matrices, echelon form, and solving systems of linear equations.
- • Understand vector spaces, basis, linear transformations, and projections crucial for AI and ML models.
- • Build calculus foundations including functions, gradients, partial derivatives, and the Jacobian and Hessian matrices.
- • Grasp key probability concepts such as conditional probability, Bayes’ theorem, random variables, and joint distributions.

MTH 201 Data Science and Machine Learning Foundations

- • Introduce core supervised learning concepts, including regression and classification.
- • Cover essential unsupervised learning methods, particularly clustering and dimensionality reduction.
- • Introduce ensemble methods and model selection strategies.
- • Provide a strong basis in evaluation metrics and best practices for validating machine learning models.

MTH 301 Advanced Deep Learning and AI Applications

- • Master the fundamentals of deep neural networks and advanced architectures (CNNs, RNNs, transformers).
- • Understand and implement generative models (VAEs, GANs) for creative AI applications.
- • Explore specialized AI topics: NLP, computer vision, and reinforcement learning.
- • Develop insight into research frontiers and future directions in AI.

Product-Image Classifier (CNN)
Flipkart’s catalog team wants to auto-categorise product images into Apparel, Electronics, and Home. You train a small CNN on a public Indian e-commerce image dataset using free Colab GPU, push validation accuracy past 85%, and produce a confusion-matrix breakdown that names exactly which category pairs the model still fumbles — and why.

Computer vision
TensorFlow / PyTorch
CNN architecture
Colab GPU
Confusion-matrix analysis

DECISION OUTPUT
Trained classifier with documented confusion patterns the catalog team can route to manual review.

Customer-Churn Predictor & API
Build a churn-prediction model for a D2C customer dataset. Engineer RFM features (recency, frequency, monetary), train a gradient-boosting classifier, deploy as a FastAPI endpoint, and write the model card — including the ethical considerations around retention targeting that any real product team will probe in your interview.

XGBoost
RFM feature engineering
FastAPI deployment
Model card
Ethical framing

DECISION OUTPUT
A deployable churn predictor with a documented model card you can defend to a hiring panel.

## FAQs

### Program Overview

**Q: What is the program duration?**

The Minor in Artificial Intelligence & Data Science program offered by CCE, IIT Mandi, spans 9 months, divided into three trimesters. Throughout this period, students engage in a comprehensive curriculum that includes more than 50 live classes, practical sessions, and the completion of  projects. The program also incorporates evaluations, which would help students to analyze the learnings from the class. This structured approach ensures a balanced blend of theoretical knowledge and practical application, equipping students with industry-relevant skills in computer science and emerging technologies.

**Q: Will I receive a digital or physical copy of the programme certificate?**

Upon successful completion of the program and fulfillment of all academic requirements, participants will receive a physical copy of the certificate. The physical certificate will be dispatched to the registered address of the participant.

**Q: When will I receive my certificate after completing the program?**

Certificates will be issued within 2–3 months from the date of the final examination. The exact timeline for issuance will be communicated during the program.

**Q: What is the mode of delivery for the program?**

The mode of delivery of the program is online through the Zoom platform.

**Q: Who will be teaching the classes in this program?**

The Minor in Artificial Intelligence & Data Science program offered by CCE, IIT Mandi, is delivered by a distinguished team comprising Faculty members and Teaching Assistants from IIT Mandi and seasoned industry professionals. This collaborative approach ensures that students receive a robust academic foundation coupled with practical insights, preparing them for real-world challenges in the AI and data science domains.

**Q: What is campus immersion?**

The 3-day campus immersion is an optional in-person academic experience designed to enhance learning through instructor interaction, campus exposure and networking opportunities. It will also feature a pitch deck presentation, with participation from the Leadership team of CCE IIT Mandi.

**Q: Is campus immersion mandatory, and when does it take place?**

It is typically scheduled towards the end of the program or after completion. While not mandatory, participation is highly recommended. The schedule will be shared in advance to enable travel planning.

**Q: What is the fee for the campus immersion?**

The campus immersion fee is ₹800 + GST per participant, per day. This fee covers only the arrangements at the immersion venue, including lunch and refreshments provided during the scheduled immersion hours.<br><br>The immersion fee is not included in the programme fee and must be paid separately by participants. Accommodation and travel expenses are not included and must be arranged and borne individually by the participants.

### Eligibility & Admissions

**Q: What are the eligibility criteria for the program?**

Anyone with a minimum qualification of 12th grade (or equivalent) and a strong interest in building a career in Artificial Intelligence and Data Science is eligible to apply for the program.

**Q: Can international students apply for the program?**

Yes, international students can apply, provided they meet the eligibility criteria set for the course.

### Fees

**Q: What is the difference between Registration fee and Course fees?**

The Registration Fee secures your seat in the program. The Course Fee is the total program cost, which can be paid upfront or in EMIs with support from our NBFC partner. Your enrollment is confirmed only after the full Course Fee is paid.

**Q: How soon will I receive the receipt after paying the registration or course fee?**

The receipt will be emailed to your registered address within 24–48 business hours of payment.

**Q: Are there any specific financial institutions/partners involved in the EMI process?**

Yes, Masai School, the Ed-tech and implementation partner for this program is associated with trusted NBFCs like Bajaj Finance, ShopSe, Varthana, Propelld, to support you with the EMI process.

**Q: What is the fee payment deadline?**

The fee payment deadline is available on your dashboard. Kindly ensure to complete the fee payment within the timelines to complete your enrollment in the program.

**Q: What is the rate of interest on the loan?**

The rate of interest depends upon multiple factors like tenure, amount, credit score, NBFC partner, etc. The details will be informed by the finance team for the program.

### Class Schedule

**Q: What are the class timings?**

The classes are scheduled on weekdays as well as weekends, designed with your time and priorities in mind. On weekdays, classes are scheduled in the evening tentatively from 8:00 PM, and weekend timings are subject to the professor's availability and schedule. The detailed schedule will be shared with you on Day 1 of the program.

**Q: How many hours of engagement is required per week?**

You'll need to dedicate approximately 8-10 hours per week, which includes attending live sessions, completing tutorials, and engaging in self-paced study to stay on track with the program.

**Q: How will the class schedule be communicated to students?**

The class schedule will be communicated primarily through the Learning Management System (LMS). It will also be shared on the official WhatsApp community.

**Q: What if I miss a live session, will I be able to access the recording?**

We recommend attending live sessions. In any unforeseen circumstances, if you miss a live class, recordings will be made accessible on the Learning Management System (LMS). Kindly ensure to watch the entire class recording to avoid missing important concepts covered.

**Q: I am a working professional / college student and would like to plan my week ahead. Can I get access to the schedule well in advance?**

Yes, you'll receive the class schedule and updates every Saturday for the upcoming week, allowing you to plan your time effectively and stay organized.

**Q: Is there any attendance criteria in this program for course completion?**

Yes, to be eligible for course completion, you must maintain a minimum of 65% attendance, which can be achieved through either live sessions or recorded lectures.

**Q: How is attendance calculated?**

Attendance is calculated automatically through the Learning Management System (LMS). You will be marked present if you complete either one of the following in full:<br><br><strong>Live Attendance</strong> – Counted when you join the live session in full using your registered email ID and attend the class.<br><br><strong>Recording Attendance</strong> – Counted when you watch the session recording in full within 30 days of the class.

**Q: What happens if the student doesn’t meet the required attendance criteria?**

If a student does not meet the required attendance criteria, they will not be eligible to receive the course completion certificate.

**Q: What if I am unable to attend live sessions or watch recordings within 30 days due to some exigencies?**

In such cases, you can raise a support ticket with your respective Program Coordinator, along with the necessary supporting documents. Exceptions may be granted after reviewing your request.

Once your enrollment is complete, you will be onboarded on the Learning Management System (LMS). You can raise your queries through ticketing support and program coordinator support for all non-conceptual queries during the tenure of the course.

**Q: How can I interact with my peers?**

Campus events are a great opportunity to connect with your peers, exchange ideas, collaborate, and learn from each other. You can also contribute to Discussions on the Learning Management System (LMS) to collaborate and learn from each other.

**Q: Is there a helpline session for student queries?**

Yes, a dedicated live helpline session is available to address your queries. Details of the session will be shared as part of your weekly schedule to help you stay informed and supported throughout the program.

**Q: What learning resources will be made available during the program?**

You will have access to a variety of learning resources, such as notes, pre-reads, class recordings, and assignments to support learning on the Learning Management System (LMS).

**Q: How often will assessments be conducted?**

Assessments will be conducted periodically throughout the program, with evaluations at the end of each module and ongoing project work to track your progress.

**Q: What happens if I don't clear a module?**

You will be given one attempt for evaluation. The minimum criteria to clear the program is 30%, and detailed weightages for each evaluation will be shared with the grading policy during the Program Overview session.
