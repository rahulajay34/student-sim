## About FITT IIT Delhi

The Foundation for Innovation and Technology Transfer (FITT) is the industry interface and technology transfer arm of the Indian Institute of Technology Delhi. Established in 1992, FITT works to translate cutting-edge academic research into real-world impact by enabling collaboration between IIT Delhi and industry, government, investors, and entrepreneurs.

### Innovation Lifecycle Support:

FITT supports the full innovation lifecycle. Its work spans intellectual property creation and licensing, research and development partnerships, consulting and skill development, and the incubation and acceleration of technology-driven startups. FITT also manages the Research and Innovation Park at IIT Delhi, which today hosts around 100 startups across sectors such as clean energy, mobility, defence, healthcare, AI and IoT, advanced materials, and digital technologies.

### Impact & Ecosystem:

Over the years, FITT has enabled more than 2200 IIT Delhi ventures, built a portfolio of over 450 startups, and incubated more than 200 companies, including 50+ faculty-led startups. Through structured programmes covering ideation, validation, pilots, and acceleration, FITT connects innovators with funding agencies, corporates, investors, and global partners.By bridging research and market needs, FITT plays a central role in strengthening India’s deep-tech and innovation ecosystem.

## FAQs

### Program Overview

**Q: What is the Pre-incubation and Entrepreneurship leveraging AI Program from FITT, IIT Delhi about?**

The program is designed to help learners build and launch their own real startup MVP using AI and no-code tools. It equips participants to navigate every step of the entrepreneurial journey—from identifying opportunities and validating ideas to building, launching, and pitching a digital product. Learners gain hands-on exposure to startup fundamentals, Generative AI workflows, no-code development, financial modeling, and investor pitching, culminating in a functional MVP and pitch deck.

**Q: What is the program duration?**

The program spans 4 months, with an average commitment of 10 hours per week.

**Q: What is the mode of delivery for the program?**

The program is delivered online.

### Fees & Payment

**Q: What is the difference between the Registration fee and the Program fees?**

The Registration Fee secures your seat in the program, while the Program Fee is the total program fee, which can be paid in full upfront or through EMIs with the help of a financing partner.

**Q: When do I receive the receipt after paying the fee?**

The receipt will be emailed to you within 24-48 business hours after the payment is processed.

**Q: Are there any specific financial institutions/partners involved in the EMI process?**

Yes, we have partnered with trusted NBFCs like Bajaj Finance, Propelld, and Varthana to support you with EMI options for fee payment.

**Q: What is the fee payment deadline?**

The fee payment deadline is available on your dashboard. Please ensure to complete the payment within the given timeline to confirm your enrollment in the program.

**Q: What is the rate of interest on the loan?**

The interest rate for the loan depends on factors such as loan tenure, loan amount, and credit score. The loan processing team will provide you with detailed information based on the financing partner.

### Sessions Schedule

**Q: What are the session timings?**

The sessions are scheduled on weekdays as well as weekends, designed with your time and priorities in mind. On weekdays, sessions are scheduled from 8:00 PM onwards, and weekend timings are subject to the instructor's availability and schedule. The detailed schedule will be shared with you on Day 1 of the program.

**Q: How many hours of engagement are required per week?**

You will need to dedicate approximately 10 hours per week for live sessions, tutorials, and self-paced study.

**Q: How will the session schedule be communicated to participants?**

The session schedule will be shared via the Learning Management System (LMS) and will also be posted on the official WhatsApp community.

**Q: What if I miss a live session? Will I be able to access the recording?**

Yes, the recordings of all live sessions will be available on the LMS. It is highly recommended to attend the live sessions, but you can catch up on missed sessions by watching the recordings.

**Q: Is attendance mandatory for Program completion?**

While attending live sessions is strongly recommended, you can still complete the program by watching the recorded sessions. Consistent participation is important for successful program completion and certification.

### Participant Support & Learning Experience

**Q: How can I raise any queries once I enroll in the program?**

You can raise queries via the LMS ticketing support system or contact your Program Coordinator for non-conceptual questions during the program.

**Q: How can I interact with my peers?**

You will collaborate with your peers through group projects, allowing you to exchange ideas, collaborate, and learn from one another.

**Q: Is there a helpline session for participant queries?**

Yes, a dedicated helpline session is available to address your queries. The details will be shared as part of your weekly schedule.

**Q: What learning resources will be available during the program?**

You will have access to lecture notes, pre-reads, class recordings, and assignments through the LMS, supporting your learning experience.

**Q: How often will assessments be conducted?**

Assessments will be conducted periodically, with evaluations at the end of each module and ongoing project work to track your progress.

### Campus Immersion

**Q: What is campus immersion?**

The 3-day campus immersion is an optional in-person immersive experience designed to enhance learning through instructor interaction, campus exposure and networking opportunities. It will also feature a pitch deck presentation, with participation from the Leadership team of FITT.

**Q: Is campus immersion mandatory and when does it take place?**

It is typically scheduled towards the end of the program or after completion. While not mandatory, participation is highly recommended. The schedule will be shared in advance to enable travel planning.

**Q: What is the fee for the campus immersion?**

The campus immersion fee is ₹2,000 + GST per participant, per day. This fee covers only the arrangements at the immersion venue, including lunch and refreshments provided during the scheduled immersion hours.
The immersion fee is not included in the programme fee and must be paid separately by participants. Accommodation and travel expenses are not included and must be arranged and borne individually by the participants.

**Q: Will I receive a digital or physical copy of the programme certificate?**

Yes. Upon successful completion of the program and fulfillment of all requirements, participants will receive a physical copy of the certificate. The physical certificate will be dispatched to the registered address shared during enrollment.

**Q: When will I receive my certificate after completing the program?**

Certificates will be issued within 2–3 months from the date of the final examination. The exact timeline for issuance will be communicated during the program.
