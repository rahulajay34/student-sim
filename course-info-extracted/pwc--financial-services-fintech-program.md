The program provides a comprehensive foundation in finance, data analytics, and fintech, combining core financial concepts with modern digital tools. It covers financial statement analysis, programming for finance, digital operations, regulatory compliance, and emerging technologies such as AI and blockchain. # Module 1: Foundational Finance & Markets

- Construction and analysis of core Financial Statements (P&L, Balance Sheet, Cash Flow)
- Corporate Finance essentials including Time Value of Money and Risk-Return trade-offs
- Overview of Financial Markets and instruments like Equity, Debt, and Derivatives

# Module 2: Core Data & Programming Toolkit

- Advanced Excel techniques for Financial Modeling, Budgeting, and Sensitivity Analysis
- Python programming fundamentals and data analysis using the Pandas library
- Database management essentials and SQL querying for financial data

# Module 3: Digital Operations & Essential Compliance

- End-to-end Digital Operations cycles including Procure-to-Pay (P2P) and Order-to-Cash (O2C)
- Record-to-Report (R2R) processes, reconciliation, and monthly closing
- Regulatory frameworks covering KYC, AML, Digital Audit, and GST compliance

# Module 4: FinTech, AI, and Governance Strategy

- Deep dive into the FinTech ecosystem, InsurTech, and modern Digital Payment systems
- Future tech applications including Blockchain, DeFi, and Smart Contracts
- Strategic implementation of AI, Generative AI, and Cybersecurity in finance

# Module 5: Integrated Capstone Project

- End-to-end execution of a real-world industry challenge
- Application of Finance, Compliance, and Tech skills to build strategic solutions
- Final project defense and presentation

# Ask Me Anything Session (2 Hours)

An open forum session to address participant queries and facilitate discussion on topics covered throughout the training program. This session will be led by PwC experts and is intended to reinforce learning and clarify concepts as part of the curriculum.

## FAQs

### Program Overview

**Q: What is the program duration?**

The program spans 6 months, offering a structured and immersive learning journey designed to equip you with in-demand financial skills.

**Q: Will I receive a digital or physical copy of the programme certificate?**

Upon successful completion of the program and fulfilment of all academic requirements, participants will receive a digital copy of the certificate.

**Q: When will I receive my certificate after completing the program?**

Certificates will be issued within 2-3 months from the date of the final examination. The exact timeline for issuance will be communicated during the program.

**Q: What is the mode of delivery for the program?**

The mode of delivery of the program is online through the Zoom platform.

**Q: Who will be teaching the classes in this program?**

The sessions will be led by expert faculty from PwC India and supported by industry practitioners. You'll learn directly from finance specialists with deep experience in both corporate and academic domains.

### Eligibility & Admissions

**Q: What are the eligibility criteria for the program?**

The program is open to beginners. No prior experience in finance is required, making it suitable for students, early professionals, and individuals from both tech and non-tech backgrounds.

**Q: Can international students apply for the program?**

Yes, international students can apply for the program, provided they meet the eligibility criteria set for the course.

**Q: What is the admission process?**

The admissions process begins with signing up and taking a Qualifier test. If you clear the test, you'll be invited for a one-on-one session where we'll walk you through the program overview and share the next steps to help you complete your enrollment smoothly.

**Q: What skills does the Qualifier test assess?**

The Qualifier test evaluates your Logical Reasoning & Analytical Ability, Quantitative Aptitude, Verbal Ability & Communication, and basic Business & FinTech Awareness.

**Q: Can I take the Qualifier test after my college exams are over?**

Seats are limited, so we strongly recommend taking the Qualifier test at the earliest to avoid missing the opportunity to kickstart your career in fintech.

### Fees

**Q: What is the difference between the Registration fee and the Course fees?**

The Registration Fee secures your seat in the program. The Course Fee is the total program cost, which can be paid upfront or in EMIs with support from our NBFC partner. Your enrollment is confirmed only after the full Course Fee is paid.

**Q: How soon will I receive the receipt after paying the registration or course fee?**

The receipt will be emailed to your registered address within 24–48 business hours of payment.

**Q: Are there any specific financial institutions/partners involved in the EMI process?**

Yes, we've partnered with trusted NBFCs like Bajaj Finance, ShopSe, Varthana, Propelld, to support you with the EMI process.

**Q: What is the fee payment deadline?**

The fee payment deadline is available on your dashboard. Kindly ensure to complete the fee payment within the timelines to complete your enrolment in the program.

**Q: What is the rate of interest on the loan?**

The rate of interest depends upon multiple factors like tenure, amount, credit score, NBFC partner, etc. The details will be provided by the finance team for the program.

### Class Schedule

**Q: What are the class timings?**

The classes are scheduled on weekdays as well as weekends, designed with your time and priorities in mind. On weekdays, classes are scheduled from 8:00PM onwards and weekend timings are subject to the professor's availability and schedule. The detailed schedule will be shared with you on Day 1 of the program.

**Q: How many hours of engagement is required per week?**

You'll need to dedicate approximately 8-10 hours per week, which includes attending live sessions, completing tutorials, and engaging in self-paced study to stay on track with the program.

**Q: How will the class schedule be communicated to students?**

The class schedule will be communicated primarily through the Learning Management System (LMS). It will also be shared on the official WhatsApp community.

**Q: What if I miss a live session, will I be able to access the recording?**

We recommend you attend Live sessions. In any foreseen circumstances, if you miss attending live classes, recordings will be made accessible to you on the Learning Management System (LMS). Kindly ensure to watch the entire class recording to ensure you don't miss important concepts covered.

**Q: I am a working professional / college student and would like to plan my week ahead. Can I get access to the schedule well in advance?**

Yes, you'll receive the class schedule and updates every Saturday for the upcoming week, allowing you to plan your time effectively and stay organized.

**Q: Is Attendance mandatory for course completion?**

While we strongly recommend attending all live sessions to make the most of the program. In cases where you miss live classes, it's essential to watch the complete class recordings to stay on track and ensure you grasp the concepts covered. Consistent participation is important for successful course completion and getting the best learning experience.

### Student Support

**Q: How can I raise any queries once I enroll in the program?**

Once your enrolment is complete, you will be onboarded on the Learning Management System (LMS). You can raise your queries through ticketing support and your program coordinator support with all non-conceptual queries during the tenure of the course.

**Q: How can I interact with my peers?**

You can talk to other students using the chat tool inside our LMS. It’s built right in, so it’s easy to use.

**Q: Is there a helpline session for student queries?**

Yes, a dedicated live helpline session is available to address your queries. Details of the session will be shared as part of your weekly schedule to help you stay informed and supported throughout the program.

**Q: What learning resources will be made available during the program?**

You will have access to a variety of learning resources, like notes, pre-reads, class recordings, and assignments to support learning on the Learning Management System (LMS).

**Q: How often will assessments be conducted?**

Assessments will be conducted periodically throughout the program, with evaluations at the end of each module and ongoing project work to track your progress.

### Placement Opportunities

Yes. Learners who score 7+ CGPA or above and maintain a minimum of 65% attendance throughout the program will be eligible for placement opportunities provided by Masai School, the ed-tech and implementation partner for this program.
