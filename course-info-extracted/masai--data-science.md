## FAQs

**Q: What is the medium of instruction?**

The medium of instruction at Masai is English. All lectures are delivered in English. As part of the curriculum, we also help you develop your written and verbal communication skills that help you become more employable.

**Q: Do you offer loaner laptops for prospective students?**

Not at the moment.

**Q: Does college CGPA play any role in the program?**

No, your college CGPA does not play any role in your entry to our courses.

**Q: Will I receive a degree or certificate when I graduate from Masai?**

We do issue a certificate of completion upon graduation, and you also receive certification from the National Skill Development Corporation. But, Masai is not a degree institution and we do not grant Bachelors or Masters Degrees. The core focus at Masai is on skill development and holistic learning. With that in mind, we have not tied up with any education institution for certification. We believe skills > degrees.

**Q: What do I do if I don't have a Pan Card/Adhaar Card?**

At the time of admission, having an Aadhaar and PAN Card is mandatory.

**Q: What do you look for in candidates when they apply to Masai?**

Masai is an extensive & immersive online education experience. It's important to ask yourself, "Is this right for me?" Below are a few of the qualities it will take to be successful in our courses: <br> <br> <ul class="pl-[25px] list-disc"><li>Determination</li> <br> <li>Proactiveness</li> <br> <li>Career-focussed</li> <br> <br> </ul><strong>Are you a self-learner? </strong><br> <br>  Learning to code or design is only one piece of the puzzle. You'll rarely be shown how to solve problems in the workplace, so Masai students graduate with the ability to autonomously tackle unique problems wherever they face them.

**Q: What is the course fee?**

You can learn at INR 0 upfront fees with Masai. You only pay us after you get placed over a certain CTC. To understand our Pay After Placement(PAP) model of course fee, you can click here and visit our <a href="https://www.masaischool.com/fees/" rel="noreferrer" target="_blank" class="text-blue-600 dark:text-blue-500 underline">Fees & PAP page</a>

**Q: The course hours don't work for me. Can I attend classes in my own time?**

No. You are required to attend classes in the defined time slot and are required to maintain 90% attendance for all our courses. If for any reason you are not able to attend your classes during your course, with prior permission, recorded lectures will be made available to you. However, please note, it won't be as effective as learning in an interactive, simulated environment.

**Q: Where is Masai located?**

The Masai head office is located in Bengaluru, India. All our courses are held online with a simulated learning environment, designed for interactive learning.

**Q: Is attendance mandatory?**

Yes, a minimum of 90% attendance is required to complete our courses. You also need to follow the guidelines mentioned in Masai's Code of Conduct Policy. * <br> <br> *Relaxations are provided to part time programs (working professionals and final year students)

<b>Masai One </b> offers <b>tech courses </b> in Software Development and Data Science, designed for beginners with no prior technical experience. These courses equip students with the skills needed to build a career in the tech industry. <br/> <br/> <b>Masai Launchpad </b> focuses on <b> non-tech courses </b> like Digital Marketing and Business Analytics. These programs are also beginner-friendly and require no prior experience, helping learners build expertise in high-demand business domains.
