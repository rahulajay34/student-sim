Week 1: Foundations of Generative Artificial Intelligence for Business

- • What makes Generative AI different from traditional analytics and automation
- • Evolution from basic ML → deep learning → NLP → LLMs
- • How LLMs work conceptually (tokens, transformers, next-token prediction)
- • LLM capabilities and limitations in enterprise settings

Week 2: Decision Support and Value Realization with Large Language Models

- • LLMs as decision copilots: decision briefs, structured outputs, and rationale generation
- • Scenario planning and sensitivity analysis with GenAI
- • KPI-driven value measurement and ROI estimation
- • Adoption models: workflows, change management, and decision governance

Week 3: Autonomous Supply Chain Systems: Design Principles and Governance

- • Autonomy maturity models and decision delegation frameworks
- • Human-in-the-loop automation, exception handling, and escalation design
- • Guardrails, auditability, compliance constraints, and monitoring
- • Operational risk controls for autonomous decision systems

Week 4: Simulation-Based Analysis of Supply Chain Coordination with Generative AI

- • Bullwhip effect, information delays, and oscillations
- • Coordination mechanisms: shared forecasts, centralized reasoning, distributed control
- • GenAI-assisted policy testing through simulation
- • Performance evaluation: service, cost, stability, and recovery time

Week 5: Generative AI for Optimization-Driven Decision-Making

- • Optimization formulation: objectives, constraints, feasibility, and sensitivity analysis
- • GenAI as a natural-language interface to optimization modelling
- • Decision validation through solver-backed guardrails
- • RAG pipelines

Week 6: Enterprise Deployment and Operationalization of Generative AI Decision Systems

- • Enterprise architecture: RAG + tool-use + orchestration + execution integration
- • Scaling patterns: caching, batching, routing across model sizes
- • Memory-constrained inference and online scheduling of LLM workloads
- • Observability and governance: monitoring, audit trails, drift detection, incident response
- • Frameworks: LangChain / LangGraph, LlamaIndex, evaluation harnesses

## FAQs

### Programme Overview

**Q: What is the duration of the program?**

The program spans 6 weeks, offering a structured and comprehensive learning experience.

**Q: What topics and concepts are covered in the program?**

It focuses on applying Generative AI and decision intelligence to enterprise decision-making, covering LLM-driven decision support, supply chain autonomy, simulation, optimization, and revenue-focused business use cases.

**Q: Will I receive a certificate upon completion?**

Yes. Upon successful completion of the program and fulfillment of all academic requirements, participants will receive a digital copy of the certificate on their registered email Id.

**Q: When will I receive my certificate after completing the program?**

Certificates will be issued within 2–3 months from the date of the final examination. The exact timeline for issuance will be communicated during the program.

**Q: What is the mode of delivery for the program?**

The classes will be conducted online through a dedicated platform, and access details will be shared on or before the batch start date.

**Q: Who will be teaching the classes in this program?**

The sessions will be led by esteemed faculty members from MIT and IIM, who bring deep expertise in Generative AI, business decision-making.

**Q: Will I work on real-world case studies?**

You will learn through real-world case studies that will be solved live during the class.

### Eligibility & Admissions

**Q: What are the eligibility criteria for the program?**

Anyone with a minimum qualification of 12th grade (or equivalent) and a strong interest in building a career in data is eligible to apply for the program.

**Q: Can international students apply for the program?**

Yes, international students can apply for the program, provided they meet the eligibility criteria.

**Q: What is the eligibility to receive the certificate?**

To be eligible to receive the certificate from IIM Mumbai, participants must successfully complete the program by maintaining a minimum of 75% attendance and completing at least 3 out of the 5 quizzes. The certificate is awarded based on overall performance and fulfillment of the program's completion criteria, in line with IIM Mumbai's high academic standards.

**Q: Is Attendance Mandatory for Course Completion?**

Yes, attendance is mandatory for course completion. Participants are required to maintain a minimum of 75% attendance in the live sessions to be eligible for the certificate. Regular participation ensures a thorough understanding of the course material and aligns with the academic standards upheld by IIM Mumbai.

### Fees & Payment

**Q: What is the difference between Registration fee and Course fees?**

The Registration Fee secures your seat in the program. The Course Fee is the total program cost, which can be paid upfront. Your enrollment is confirmed only after the full Course Fee is paid.

**Q: How soon will I receive the receipt after paying the registration or course fee?**

The receipt will be emailed to your registered address within 24–48 business hours of payment.

**Q: What is the fee payment deadline?**

The fee payment deadline is available on your dashboard. Kindly ensure to complete the fee payment within the timelines to complete your enrolment in the program.

### Class Schedule

**Q: What are the class timings?**

Classes are scheduled on weekends, thoughtfully designed to suit your time and priorities. Sessions are conducted on Saturday and Sunday from 5:00 PM – 7:30 PM, ensuring a convenient and focused learning experience.

**Q: How many hours of engagement is required per week?**

You'll need to dedicate approximately 6-7 hours per week, which includes attending five hours of live sessions, completing tutorials, and engaging in self-paced study to stay on track with the program.

**Q: How will the class schedule be communicated to students?**

The class schedule will be communicated primarily through the email on your registered email ID.

**Q: What if I miss a live session, will I be able to access recording?**

We recommend you to attend Live sessions. In any foreseen circumstances if you miss attending live classes, recordings will be made accessible to you. Kindly ensure to watch the entire class recording to ensure you don't miss important concepts covered.

**Q: I am a working professional / college student and would like to plan my week ahead. Can I get access to the schedule well in advance?**

Yes, you'll receive the class schedule and updates well in advance, allowing you to plan your time effectively and stay organized.

**Q: Is there any attendance criteria in this program for course completion?**

Yes, to be eligible for course completion, you must maintain a minimum of 75% attendance, which can be achieved through live sessions.

**Q: How is attendance calculated?**

Attendance is calculated automatically through the Learning Management System (LMS). Live Attendance – Counted when you join the live session in full using your registered email ID and attend the class.

**Q: What happens if the student doesn't meet the required attendance criteria?**

If a student does not meet the required attendance criteria, they will not be eligible to receive the course completion certificate.
