### Projects

Onboarding Funnel Audit & Wireframe
Take PhonePe’s public onboarding flow — download from the Play Store, screenshot every step. Map the funnel, identify five friction points using public reviews as evidence, write a one-pager prioritising three fixes by impact-vs-effort, and ship Figma wireframes for the top fix. This is what a Product Analyst portfolio piece actually looks like.

User-journey mapping
Friction analysis
Impact-effort prioritisation
Figma wireframes
Voice-of-customer mining

DECISION OUTPUT
A prioritised three-fix recommendation with wireframes ready for design review.

AI Personalised-Stylist Spec
Write the complete spec for an AI-powered ‘Personalised Stylist’ feature on Myntra. Define the ML/LLM evaluation approach, the data needs, fallback behaviour when the model is uncertain, ethical considerations (recommendation diversity, body positivity), and the rollout plan. Then defend it in a live panel review — the closest dress rehearsal for a Senior PM interview loop.

AI product specification
ML evaluation literacy
Ethics & fallbacks
Rollout planning
Panel defence

DECISION OUTPUT
A defendable AI-feature spec ready for cross-functional review with engineering, design, and legal.

## FAQs

### Program Overview

**Q: What is the Product Management with Applied AI from iHUB DivyaSampark, IIT Roorkee about?**

The Future-Ready Product Management with Applied AI Program by iHUB DivyaSampark, IIT Roorkee is a 6-month online program designed to help learners master product management in the era of AI. The course combines strategic product thinking and applied AI/agent-driven innovation, enabling students to build real-world AI-powered products. Learners benefit from live classes, academic support, case-based learning, hands-on tool experience, and a capstone project, with guidance from IIT faculty and industry experts.

**Q: What is the program duration?**

The program duration is 6 months, with a weekly time commitment of 8-10 hours.

**Q: Will I receive a digital or physical copy of the programme certificate?**

Upon successful completion of the program and fulfillment of all requirements, participants will receive a digital and physical copy of the certificate. The physical certificate will be dispatched to the registered address shared during enrollment.

**Q: When will I receive my certificate after completing the program?**

Certificates will be issued within 2–3 months from the date of the final examination. The exact timeline for issuance will be communicated during the program.

**Q: What is the mode of delivery for the program?**

The mode of delivery of the program is online through the Zoom platform.

**Q: Who will be teaching the classes in this program?**

Classes are delivered by professors from IIMs, IITs, senior product managers from top tech firms, and experienced product managers who host weekly doubt-clearing labs.

**Q: What is campus immersion?**

The 3-day campus immersion is an optional in-person immersive experience designed to enhance learning through instructor interaction, campus exposure and networking opportunities.

**Q: Is campus immersion mandatory, and when does it take place?**

It is typically scheduled towards the end of the program or after completion. While not mandatory, participation is highly recommended. The schedule will be shared in advance to enable travel planning.

**Q: What is the fee for the campus immersion?**

The campus immersion fee is ₹1,200 + GST per participant, per day. This fee covers only the arrangements at the immersion venue, including lunch and refreshments provided during the scheduled immersion hours.<br><br>The immersion fee is not included in the programme fee and must be paid separately by participants.<br><br>Limited accommodation for girls/female participants may be arranged subject to availability. Boys/male participants will need to arrange their accommodation independently. Accommodation and Travel expenses must be borne by students and are not included in the campus immersion fees.

**Q: What made you interested in this specific program? What made you specifically interested in product management with applied AI?**

Learners often choose this program to pivot into AI-enhanced product roles, gain an IIT-endorsed credential, and build a portfolio that differentiates them in the job market.

### Eligibility & Admissions

**Q: What are the eligibility criteria for the program?**

Anyone with a minimum qualification of 12th grade (or equivalent) and a strong interest in building a career in Product Management with applied AI is eligible to apply for the program.

**Q: Can international students apply for the program?**

Yes, international students can apply for the program, provided they meet the eligibility criteria set for the course.

**Q: What is the admission process?**

The admissions process begins with signing up and taking a Qualifier test. If you clear the test, you'll be invited for a one-on-one session where we'll walk you through the program overview and share the next steps to help you complete your enrollment smoothly.

**Q: What skills does the Qualifier test assess?**

The Qualifier test evaluates your Logical Reasoning & Data Interpretation, Verbal Ability, and Quantitative Aptitude, which are basic requirements of the program.

**Q: Can I take the Qualifier test after my college exams are over?**

Seats are limited, so we strongly recommend taking the Qualifier test at the earliest to avoid missing the opportunity to kickstart your career in Product Management with applied AI.

**Q: Will there be examinations?**

There are 6 online module exams and one capstone project which will be your final project. All details will be shared in your LMS and submission also need to be done in the LMS.
